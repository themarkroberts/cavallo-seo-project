Greg Giles, CEO of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., made the significant move from Australia to join The Cavallo Team, bringing with him a wealth of expertise in design, production, and the business of footwear. Greg’s extensive background in these areas has shaped his understanding of the critical importance of high-quality products for ensuring both longevity and customer satisfaction. His dedication to maintaining uncompromising standards has been a driving force behind Cavallo’s success in the field of barefoot hoof care.

In 1999, as Managing Director of Old Macs, Greg began his journey into hoof boot education. During this time, he was instrumental in developing and introducing Old Mac Boots to the equine industry. His hands-on experience in creating effective, high-quality hoof boots laid the foundation for his future innovations. By 2006, Greg had expanded his expertise to Cavallo, where he applied his profound knowledge in sourcing, manufacturing, commerce, and marketing to enhance Cavallo Products and elevate the Cavallo Brand.

Under Greg’s leadership, Cavallo has focused on producing the best hoof boots available, with particular attention to those required for specific conditions such as Laminitis and Navicular Disease. His commitment to natural hoof care is evident in the design and functionality of Cavallo’s products, ensuring they meet the diverse needs of horses while promoting their overall well-being. Greg is passionate about educating horse owners on how to measure hoof boots accurately to achieve a perfect fit, which is essential for maximizing the effectiveness and comfort of the hoof boots.

Greg firmly believes that building strong relationships within the equine industry is crucial to running a successful business. This approach ensures timely delivery, eliminates backorders, maintains flawless distribution, and guarantees complete end-user satisfaction. His dedication to these principles ensures that Cavallo Hoof Boots are not only high in quality but also readily available in 25 countries worldwide. Tens of thousands of horses benefit annually from the superior comfort and protection provided by Cavallo Hoof Boots, which are designed to perform well on any terrain and at any speed.

Together with Carole Herder, Greg is deeply passionate about their work and lives. They have assembled a dedicated team of hoof boot experts who share their commitment to innovation and excellence. The Cavallo Team focuses on developing and delivering products that offer unmatched comfort, protection, safety, and support for both horses and riders. Through their combined efforts, Greg and Carole continue to advance the field of barefoot hoof care, ensuring that every horse receives the best possible care and support.
