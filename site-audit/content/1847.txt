<h3>Cavallo Helps Therapeutic Horses Wear Hoof Boots!</h3>
<a href="http://cavallo.local" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">We at Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider</a> are proud to announce our continuing sponsorship of <a href="http://www.pathintl.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">PATH: the Professional Association of Therapeutic Horsemanship International.</a>  Cavallo has sponsored the organization, as well as their riding centers, for more than five years.

“Cavallo supports numerous equine charities, rescues, therapy horses and associations. However, PATH Intl. is a very special and unique organization,” says Cavallo CEO Greg Giles. “We are proud to be able to have been involved with PATH Intl. They do such a wonderful job helping people through a connection with horses.”

<h3><strong>The PATH </strong><b>International Story</b></h3>
The Professional Association of Therapeutic Horsemanship International® was formed in 1969 to promote safety and optimal outcomes in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equine-assisted_therapy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">equine-assisted activities and therapies (EAAT)</a> for individuals with special needs. Today, PATH Intl. has 873 member centers and nearly 8,000 individual members in countries all over the world, The membership supports almost 69,000 men, women, and children with special needs (including more than 6,700 veterans) each year through a variety of equine-assisted activities and therapies programs.

In addition to certification and accreditation, PATH Intl. offers many different educational resources to its members and other industry professionals. Workshops, educational publications, newsletters, online education, mentor programs and peer networking, and more!
