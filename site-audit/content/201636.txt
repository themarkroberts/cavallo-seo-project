<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Why Use Cavallo Hoof Boots for Turnout? </h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots are the ideal solution for protecting your horse’s hooves during turnout. Horses often face harsh terrain, wet or muddy conditions, which can lead to injuries, thrush, or white line disease. Cavallo boots shield your horse’s hooves from these risks, offering essential protection without compromising hoof quality, flexibility, or shock absorption. The boots provide protection while still allowing your horse to move naturally, maintaining the flexibility and functionality of their hooves.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You can also use your Cavallo Hoof Boots as a medical boot. They help keep hooves clean, hold medicine, and support recovery.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>On dry, hard ground, Cavallo Hoof Boots provide proven shock absorption. They add comfort for older horses and those with joint stiffness.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For shod horses, Cavallo Hoof Boots for Turnout help prevent pulled shoes and overreach injuries. They also support hoof health and reduce strain. With a secure fit and durable design, these boots let horses move naturally while staying protected.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Benefits of Cavallo Hoof Boots for Your Horse During Turnout</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0"><br>Cavallo Hoof Boots for Turnout go beyond basic hoof protection, offering long-term benefits for your horse’s health and movement. Unlike traditional turnout solutions, these boots provide a lightweight, breathable alternative to shoeing or wrapping/bandaging hooves. This allows for better air circulation while keeping hooves clean and protected.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Horses naturally move more during turnout, which strengthens their hooves, tendons, joints, and muscles. Cavallo Hoof Boots support this movement by absorbing shock without restricting flexibility, reducing strain on the legs over time. This makes them especially useful for horses recovering from hoof sensitivity or transitioning from shoes to barefoot.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For active horses, these boots reduce excessive wear on hooves, preventing cracking, chipping, or uneven wear patterns. They also serve as a barrier against stones and debris, keeping hooves comfortable and reducing the risk of irritation.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For horses wearing shoes, Cavallo Boots soften the impact of kicks in group turnout, preventing injuries to other horses. They also reduce concussion on hard ground, making turnout more comfortable for horses with arthritis or joint stiffness.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>With Cavallo Hoof Boots, your horse gets the freedom to move naturally while staying protected in all turnout conditions.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Using Your Cavallo Boots In Turnout for Increased Comfort and Protection </h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots for Turnout are easy to use and designed for daily wear. Start by ensuring the boots fit snugly but comfortably, allowing your horse to move naturally without rubbing or shifting. A proper fit ensures maximum protection and performance. If you need help with sizing or fitting, our <strong><a href="http://cavallo.local/how-to-measure/">Cavallo Sizing Team</a></strong> is happy to assist.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For barefoot horses, simply secure the boots using the front Velcro straps. If your horse is shod, you can place the boots over metal shoes to help prevent pulled shoes, overreach injuries, and excessive hoof wear. The boots should fit securely but not be too tight.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Regular turnout exposes boots to mud, water, and debris, so it’s important to clean them regularly. Rinse with water to remove dirt and small stones, and check daily for any signs of wear or irritation to ensure your horse’s comfort. Cavallo recommends spraying a 50/50 mix of water and apple cider vinegar on your horse’s hooves daily to help prevent bacterial buildup inside the boots. During wet winter months, use athlete’s foot powder to reduce moisture.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots stay secure during turnout, allowing your horse to run, play, and move freely. Whether your horse is recovering from an injury, transitioning to barefoot, or simply in need of extra protection, these boots provide long-lasting comfort and support.</p>
