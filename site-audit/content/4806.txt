<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Why Road Safety &amp; the Right Gear are EVERYTHING:</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size">At Cavallo, we receive a lot of unsolicited testimonials, but THIS ONE HIT HOME. This one shook us up. We can’t get it out of our heads… Please read this heartfelt message from Carolyn Gaedtke of Florida:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">“Thankful for our Cavallo Boots. Two months ago, my four boys and our three ponies were out driving when a woman decided to run a stop sign and plowed into the back of my cart. The police believe she was on her phone and estimate she hit us at about 40 mph.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Her front bumper got caught in my carriage wheel, and she pushed my pony, Anna, about 35 feet. Above is a picture of the cart. You can see the wheel where the woman’s bumper was stuck so that we couldn’t get away, and she was pushing us. My 8-year-old son was in the cart with me.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">It’s a miracle my pony, my son and I are alive! Anna’s Cavallo Boots got really beat up! If our pony hadn’t been wearing them it is highly likely her feet would have been destroyed and she would have likely had to be put down. Her boots protected her feet!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></div>
<!-- /wp:column -->

<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">The accident shredded parts of the boot bottoms and ripped the closing tabs. She was in the back and got the direct hit and was pushed over 35 feet into my boys and the pony in front of her.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Our other pony and my three boys in the other cart were only mildly injured from us being pushed into their cart and the boots protected their pony, too. It’s a miracle we are all ok and I know the boots were a big part of that.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When I looked at the boots my stomach hurt to think what if that had been her hooves!!!!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">None of the ponies panicked. All of our ponies are small, under 50 inches. I am amazed they are ok.</p>

<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">Please share in any way that might be helpful for horse people or others. If we can help or save one person from going through what we have it is worth it.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I can’t believe it’s been 9 weeks now since the accident and the ponies have been cleared by the vet and chiropractor and are driving again! I will take more time and might have permanent back pain.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We had a “SLOW-MOVING” sign on our cart with flashing red lights like for bikes, even though it wasn’t yet dark. The driver admitted she wasn’t paying attention. It is but by the grace of God my children and I and and our ponies are all alive.</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
