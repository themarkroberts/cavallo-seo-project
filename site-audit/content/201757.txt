<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="When-to-use-pads">When to use Cavallo Hoof Boot Pads</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>For Riding</strong><br>Cavallo Hoof Boot Pads add an extra layer of shock absorption and support when riding on trails, pavement, or high-impact activities. They minimize the impact on your horse’s hooves and joints, reducing discomfort caused by hard or uneven terrain.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>For Trailering or Stalling</strong><br>Horses standing for prolonged periods in trailers or stalls can benefit from adding Cavallo Hoof Boot Pads. The pads create a comfortable barrier that reduces pressure and helps absorb vibrations from hard surfaces, such as trailer floors. By providing cushioning, the pads help further alleviate strain on hooves and joints.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>For Rehabilitation, Recovery, and Therapy</strong><br>Cavallo Hoof Boot Pads are an invaluable addition for horses recovering from hoof-related conditions like laminitis, founder, navicular disease, or thin soles. They create a soft, cushioned base inside your Cavallo boot. The added comfort helps manage pain and protects sensitive hooves, offering essential support during recovery to promote healing. Not sure which type of pad you should purchase? Keep reading to learn more!</p>
