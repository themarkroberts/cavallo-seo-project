<h4><strong>A Beautiful Bond</strong></h4>
Some years ago, we noticed we had some pretty significant things in common with <a href="https://www.pathintl.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">PATH International</a>.
<p style="text-align: justify;">Firstly, we share an ultimate love and respect for horses;  with a strong belief that horses have the ability to heal people. Secondly, we believe that horses deserve to be comfortable and pain-free.  We at <a href="http://cavallo.local/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cavallo</a> are truly moved by the commitment, compassionate and generosity of spirit shown by all the staff and volunteers of PATH. These are the people who give not only of their time, but of their hearts to the work they do; healing PTSD syndrome suffered by war vets, sexually abused children, autism and many other seriously debilitating conditions, all with the kind assistance of amazing horses.  As a business and in our way of helping to give, we are proud of the generous sponsorship we have maintained with PATH over the years.</p>

So fabulous!</p>
