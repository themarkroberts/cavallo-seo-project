<p>We have changed throughout history in amazing ways. Practices and beliefs that were once conventional are now outdated. We believed the world was flat. Leeches healed all illness. Acid rain was only a myth. If you kept “making faces’ your face would stay that way forever. Nailing metal shoes on horses feet isn’t damaging. </p>
<p>And just think about the last 10 years. Everyday use of the internet! Even kids have cell phones! And here’s a real surprise – regular use of the credit card is only 20 years old – the credit card – the thing we use everyday!  I marvel at the speed of change and yet all that while our archaic metal shoeing practice remained the same.</p>
<p>Now we have genuine alternatives and freedom of choice.</p>
<p>What choice will you make? </p>
