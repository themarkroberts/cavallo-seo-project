<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Journey the Mustang - 100 Days to go from Wild to Mild</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Molly Kline was honoured to be accepted to participate in the <a href="https://extrememustangmakeover.com/events/kentucky/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">2020 Kentucky Extreme Mustang Challenge</a>. She's been given a wild mustang named Journey for 100 days to tame and train. She'll compete with Journey in the Challenge and will also work hard to find her an excellent to find her an excellent new home.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Molly noticed that Journey's feet seemed quite sore on rocky trails.&nbsp; She contacted us and we just didn't want to see Journey's natural mustang-feet shod.&nbsp; We sent them a pair of Trek Hoof Boots and welcomed the pair to Team Cavallo!</p>
