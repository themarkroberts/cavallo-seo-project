Maren is an avid equine enthusiast who is deeply passionate about horses, hooves, and holistic care. Inspired by her two beloved geldings, she ventured into the world of hoof care after discovering the challenges they faced with their feet. This journey ignited a passion for equine podiatry and bodywork, leading her to found a boutique business specializing in hoof care, bodywork, and R+ training.

In addition to her business, Maren serves as a Customer Experience Expert at Cavallo. With years of firsthand experience using Cavallo Boots on her horses, she guides customers in discovering the benefits of booted and barefoot hoof care.
