<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Why Add these Books to Your Horse Care Library?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} -->
<h5 class="wp-block-heading">Protect Your Horse</h5>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>My books emphasize protecting your horse from pain and discomfort, while encouraging alternative approaches to traditional practices, such as considering barefoot and booted hoof care, instead of metal shoes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} -->
<h5 class="wp-block-heading">Maximize Your Riding Experience</h5>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Explore new ways to enhance your riding experience and connection with your horse. By understanding your horse’s natural way of being, you can be more present and make decisions that benefit both you and your equine companion.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} -->
<h5 class="wp-block-heading">Hot Spots for Horses</h5>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Gain insights into what the ‘hot spots’ may be for your horse, backed by scientific evidence and real-life stories. Understanding these areas can help you manage your horse’s well-being more effectively.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} -->
<h5 class="wp-block-heading">Challenge Past Patterns</h5>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Challenge past ways of thinking that have impacted your horse. It's never too late... Let your curiosity lead you to explore new ideas that will flow through to positive change.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":5} -->
<h5 class="wp-block-heading">See Your Whole Horse</h5>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I take a holistic approach to understanding and caring for horses. Every aspect of a horse’s health and life is interconnected. In my books, I address not only the physical well-being of horses but also their mental health. When considering your care plan, consider your horse’s anatomy, physiology, exercise, and overall lifestyle.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If you’re passionate about your horse, these books are definitely worth exploring!</p>
