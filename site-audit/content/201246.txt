<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Cost of Traditional Shoeing vs. Barefoot &amp; Booted Hoofcare</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>One of the biggest expenses for horse owners is hoof care. Whether you ride recreationally or competitively, keeping your horse’s hooves healthy and protected is essential. However, it doesn’t have to break the bank. Many horse owners are now switching from costly traditional shoeing to a more natural, budget-friendly approach: going barefoot and using Cavallo Hoof Boots when needed. This switch can lead to significant Cavallo Hoofcare Savings while maintaining your horse’s comfort and protection.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Traditional Shoeing Costs</strong> - Horses that wear metal shoes typically require shoeing every six weeks to maintain proper hoof health and prevent discomfort or lameness. Here’s what that looks like financially:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>$200 per shoeing session</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>8 shoeing sessions per year (every 6 weeks) → $1,600 per year</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Total yearly cost: $1,600 USD</strong></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Barefoot &amp; Booted Costs</strong> - By transitioning to a barefoot lifestyle and using Cavallo Hoof Boots when needed, you can dramatically cut down on your annual hoof care expenses while keeping your horse comfortable and protected. Here’s the cost breakdown:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>$50 per barefoot trim </li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>8 trims per year (every 6 weeks) → $400 per year</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cavallo Trek Boots (designed to last approximately two years with regular use) → $229.90 per pair; $114.95 per year</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Total yearly cost: $514.95 USD</strong></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>The Savings Add Up</strong> - By choosing to go barefoot and booted, you save <strong>$1,085 per horse every year.</strong> Over the course of a horse’s lifetime, these savings can add up to tens of thousands of dollars!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Not only is this approach more affordable, but it also supports natural hoof function, improved circulation, and long-term soundness.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Rehab Costs: Another Major Cavallo Hoofcare Saving Opportunity</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">Beyond routine hoof care, rehabilitation for injuries or conditions such as laminitis, abscesses, and founder can be costly. Furthermore, traditional rehabilitation shoeing can quickly add up. With rehab shoeing costs often exceeding <strong>$300 per session and a 3- to 4-week trim cycle</strong>, expenses can escalate quickly. In some cases, <strong>annual costs can exceed $3,500 to $4,000.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots offer an affordable, effective alternative for therapeutic hoof care. They provide comfort and protection without the high costs of custom shoeing. Simply slip on a pair of Cavallo Hoof Boots for instant support, shock absorption, and reduced strain on sore hooves and joints. This offers your horse immediate relief without breaking the bank.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>One of the greatest benefits of Cavallo Hoof Boots is their versatility. Whether for stall rest, turnout, riding, or more, Cavallo Hoof Boots provide all the support your horse needs without the added cost of purchasing multiple styles. With a variety of options available, Cavallo caters to any budget. Our most affordable style, the ELB Hoof Boot, starts at just $84.95 USD, or <strong>only $169.90 for a pair!</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>By switching to Cavallo Hoof Boots instead of costly therapeutic shoeing, you can significantly reduce rehab expenses. This allows your horse to get the protection, support, and comfort they need for a successful recovery while enjoying Cavallo Hoofcare Savings.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Beyond Hoofcare Cost Savings: The Health Benefits of Cavallo Hoof Boots</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Choosing a barefoot and booted approach doesn’t just save money on hoof care. It also supports long-term hoof health and overall soundness, ultimately reducing future vet bills. Cavallo Hoof Boots provide a range of benefits that extend well beyond simple cost savings.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Unlike metal shoes, which only protect the outer edge of the hoof wall, Cavallo Hoof Boots offer complete coverage. They protect the entire hoof, including the soles and bulbs. This prevents excessive wear and provides extra support for long-term hoof health.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Moreover, the thick, durable soles of Cavallo Hoof Boots are scientifically proven to reduce concussion and impact. This not only helps prevent arthritis and lameness but also minimizes strain on joints and tendons. Whether your horse is walking on hard ground, rocky terrain, or frozen surfaces, Cavallo Hoof Boots offer unmatched comfort. They provide superior stability and traction on all types of terrain. This ensures your horse stays comfortable and secure, no matter the conditions.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots are also versatile and can be used for various activities. Whether your horse is trail riding, jumping, participating in dressage, or undergoing rehabilitation, Cavallo Hoof Boots offer the perfect protection. Cavallo Hoof Boots combine durability and affordability, making them an ideal choice for all activities. They provide the perfect solution for everyday hoof care and recovery, all while offering Cavallo Hoofcare Savings.</p>
