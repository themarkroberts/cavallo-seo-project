<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Recovery Since 2006: The Cavallo Legacy of Innovation</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size">In 2006, we pioneered the first hoof boot explicitly designed for both riding and therapy. We recognized that equine rehabilitation shouldn’t mean sidelining your connection with your horse.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">With Cavallo, horses could stay active and supported — whether they were navigating rocky terrain or recovering from Laminitis, Navicular issues, abscesses, or other hoof sensitivities!</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Since then, we’ve helped thousands of horses around the world stay sound, comfortable, and moving forward — with the peace of mind that their hooves are supported every step of the way!</p>

<p>Today, we’re proud to see more companies embracing the path we pioneered. And while others follow, we continue to lead—with confidence and clarity in our legacy.</p>
