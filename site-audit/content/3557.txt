Jenny began working with Cavallo in 2015, left for a short time to pursue other interests but felt called back and resumed her position in 2017. Jenny handles social media, marketing and growing partnerships between Cavallo and other equine industry organizations. She spends a lot of time outdoors with her dogs, on the beach or in the beautiful BC forest. She grew up with horses but has not had her own horse as an adult, so her job at Cavallo works to fill that gap!

Jenny’s job at Cavallo is tied to her passion for helping animals. Everyone at Cavallo is bonded by a deep love for animals (specifically horses), and a drive to do whatever is in their power to help horses thrive and be understood – free of pain and discomfort.
