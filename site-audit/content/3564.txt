Heidi helps Cavallo spread the word about new products and equine wellness. She works in the horse magazine and equine publishing world—and has for over 20 years. Heidi authored Western Horseman’s Understanding Lameness, Western Horseman’s Legends 6 and 9, and Goodnight’s Guide to Great Horsemanship. She writes for several nationally-circulated horse magazines and holds first-prize awards from American Horse Publications for her writing and photography.

Heidi started riding Ponies of the Americas at age five at Smiley R Ranch in Hilliard, Ohio. In college, she was president and later assistant coach of the Ohio Wesleyan University Equestrian Team. Now, she’s a registered instructor with PATH International.

Melocco resides on her small-acreage horse property with her husband, Jared, daughter, Savannah, registered AQHA gelding named “Charlie,” a pony named “Romeo,” dogs “Lucy” and “Rosie,” four friendly hens, and three orange barn kitties.
