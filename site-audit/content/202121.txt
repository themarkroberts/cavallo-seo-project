<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Why Use Cavallo Hoof Boots for Mounted Units</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Mounted police and patrol units rely on strong, sure-footed horses. Further, these horses must navigate busy streets, rough terrain, and unpredictable situations. That’s why Cavallo Hoof Boots for mounted units are a smart, reliable choice. They provide the grip, comfort, and protection needed to keep horses safe and focused.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>First and foremost, Cavallo boots offer excellent traction on a variety of surfaces. Whether on pavement, grass, gravel, or wet ground, horses stay steady. Unlike metal shoes, which often slip on slick roads, Cavallo boots increase stability and confidence. As a result, horses move through crowds or over debris with ease and precision.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Additionally, Cavallo Hoof Boots significantly reduce concussion on hard surfaces. Mounted units frequently patrol on asphalt or concrete for hours at a time. However, metal shoes send shockwaves up the horse’s leg, leading to strain and long-term damage. Cavallo boots, on the other hand, absorb shock and protect delicate structures. This greatly reduces fatigue and discomfort.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>To visualize the difference, picture yourself wearing steel boots on pavement all day. Secondly, imagine cushioned runners. That’s what horses feel with Cavallo Hoof Boots. Each step becomes easier, softer, and more natural.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Moreover, Cavallo boots flex with every stride. This supports the hoof’s natural expansion and increases blood flow throughout the leg. Improved circulation keeps horses healthier, sounder, and more willing to work long hours.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Finally, Cavallo Hoof Boots are fast and simple to use. Officers can quickly apply or remove them before or after patrol. They stay firmly in place thanks to durable materials and secure closures- even during high-speed maneuvers.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In conclusion, Cavallo Hoof Boots for mounted units offer the traction, comfort, and protection required for dependable service day in and day out.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Proven Results: Cavallo Hoof Boots in the Field</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">More and more mounted units are choosing Cavallo Hoof Boots for mounted units- and the results speak for themselves. These boots don’t just protect hooves. They also improve performance, reduce injuries, and cut long-term costs.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Take the Houston Mounted Patrol Unit, for example. Years ago, their horses suffered from recurring hoof issues and ongoing vet visits. Traditional metal shoes were a significant factor. The officers noticed that metal shoes increased concussion and restricted healthy hoof movement. Their horses were uncomfortable and often sore after long shifts.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So, in 2004, they made a change. They pulled one horse’s shoes and tried going barefoot with Cavallo boots. The difference was immediate. That one success led to a full herd transition -40 horses now work barefoot and booted.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Since then, their horses' overall health has improved dramatically. Veterinary bills have dropped by half. Fewer lameness issues mean less downtime, lower treatment costs, and happier, healthier horses.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For a closer look at how Cavallo helps reduce hoof care costs, visit our <strong><a href="http://cavallo.local/faq/cavallo-hoofcare-savings/">Cavallo Hoofcare Savings page.</a></strong> You'll find real numbers and proven success stories from horse owners and mounted patrol riders.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">Clearly, Cavallo Hoof Boots for mounted units are more than a replacement—they’re an upgrade. Mounted units across North America are proving that natural, booted hooves work better, cost less, and feel great.</p>
