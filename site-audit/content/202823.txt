<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo Hoof Boots Offer Effective Water Drainage </h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots provide excellent drainage in water and mud due to their thoughtfully engineered side slits. The patented drainage holes are built into the lower sides of each boot. Because of this, as your horse moves through water, the slits instantly allow moisture and water to drain. This prevents pooling and allows the boot to stay lightweight, even when fully submerged.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Conversely, boots that hold water can cause rubbing, heat buildup, or instability for your horse. Cavallo’s side slits easily solve this problem. By allowing water to exit quickly with every step, debris rarely gets trapped inside your horse's boots. Furthermore, this fast drainage helps prevent irritation to the hoof and keeps your horse moving freely.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Whether your horse is crossing a stream, playing in the water, or training in a wet arena, Cavallo boots don’t hold water. With Cavallo Hoof Boots, your horse stays comfortable and protected from unnecessary hoof stress!</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Using Cavallo Hoof Boots in Wet Conditions</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">Many hoof boots struggle in wet or muddy conditions due to suction issues or the weight of heavy materials. However, Cavallo Hoof Boots feature a unique cone-shaped upper that naturally minimizes the shape of your horse's hooves. As a result, this special design and drainage holes break suction naturally as your horse lifts their hoof, preventing boot loss.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Not only does this make a big difference in traction and comfort, but it also prevents the boots from being pulled off during movement through soft, muddy, or wet terrain.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Further, Cavallo’s materials are flexible yet supportive, which helps the boot maintain its shape even after repeated use in wet environments. Consequently, Riders report that their Cavallo boots perform exceptionally well in flooded pastures, on slick trails, and through deep rivers and water crossings!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">With Cavallo Hoof Boots, your horse stays confident on all terrain, including water. Moreover, you won’t need to worry about suction-related issues like twisting, dragging, or boot loss. That means more time riding and less time fixing problems!</p>
