<h2 class="wp-block-heading">How Cavallo Hoof Boots Help Arthritic Horses</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots offer game-changing comfort and support for aging or arthritic horses. With advanced sole technology, Cavallo boots cushion every step, allowing horses to move more freely, even when stiffness or joint pain might otherwise hold them back.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Arthritic horses often struggle on hard or uneven ground due to increased concussive force. Cavallo boots provide dependable traction on all terrain types - pavement, gravel, grass, or sand. This reliable grip helps prevent slipping, which can worsen joint damage. With better footing, horses move with greater confidence and ease.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Most importantly, Cavallo boots reduce harmful concussion. Hard surfaces cause shock to travel through the hooves and into the joints. Over time, this repeated impact contributes to inflammation, pain, and reduced mobility. Cavallo’s shock-absorbing soles soften each step, easing strain on sensitive joints and hooves.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>As horses move more comfortably, circulation improves, which is key for managing arthritis. Gentle, regular movement helps lubricate joints and increases blood flow. Cavallo boots support this healing cycle by encouraging pain-free activity. Movement matters whether turned out in the paddock or going for light rides. Cavallo supports both physical comfort and emotional well-being.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Cavallo boots are also easy to use, even for owners with limited hand strength. The quick closures make booting up fast and simple, with no tools or bending required. That means more time enjoying your horse—not struggling with gear or lifting legs for too long.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Whether managing early arthritis or keeping a senior horse active, Cavallo Hoof Boots for arthritis provide the comfort, traction, and protection your horse needs to keep moving happily.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Science Behind Cavallo Hoof Boots for Arthritis Relief</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">Independent research confirms what horse owners have known for years: Cavallo Hoof Boots for arthritis reduce pressure, absorb shock, and improve traction. All critical factors in supporting arthritic horses.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>A study by Western Kentucky University’s Equine Science Department compared Cavallo Trek Hoof Boots with traditional metal horseshoes and barefoot hooves. Using FujiFilm pressure-mapping technology, scientists measured force, pressure, and hoof contact on hard surfaces like asphalt. The results were clear - Cavallo boots outperformed both metal shoes and barefoot.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Cavallo boots significantly reduced pressure on the hoof and helped absorb concussive force. For arthritic horses, this matters. Impact from hard ground can intensify joint inflammation and pain. By softening every step, Cavallo boots protect not only the hoof but the entire limb.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Researchers also noted increased ground contact, thanks to Cavallo’s flexible, thick tread. More surface contact equals better grip and improved weight distribution. This helps arthritic horses move more naturally and with less risk of slipping. That steadiness means horses are more willing to stay active, even on rough or uneven ground.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>The study, published in the <em>Journal of Equine Veterinary Science</em>, gives scientific credibility to what horse owners already feel in the saddle and see in the paddock.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">In short, Cavallo Hoof Boots go far beyond basic protection. Whether your horse is recovering, aging, or managing chronic arthritis, Cavallo offers scientifically-backed support and comfort that helps keep them moving with ease.</p>
