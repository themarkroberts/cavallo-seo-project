<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Cheyenne Wimberley is Heading to the National Finals Rodeo with Trek!</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cheyenne Wimberly is an accomplished barrel racer and three-time National Finals Rodeo Qualifier (WOW!)!&nbsp; She's heading out on the road again and will compete at the <a href="https://www.nfrexperience.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">National Finals Rodeo (NFR)</a> in Las Vegas, this December!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Trailering with Trek</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cheyenne recently got in touch with us. She's happy to offer her hard-working horse <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/trek-hoof-boots/?fbclid=IwAR1InX3B7xXBtMbpkEiHYjNBfA407o9NYHZ7fCOg4SKllebh0u6Mz4R4SPI" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cavallo Trek Hoof Boots</a> to offer comfort and fight fatigue while trailering.&nbsp; Her horse enjoys Trek with <a href="http://cavallo.local/product/comfort-pad/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Comfort Pads</a> added (cushy pads that increase comfort on the road and reduce fatigue).&nbsp; Cheyenne also pops in <a href="http://cavallo.local/product/high-tenacity-protection-pads/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Protection Pads</a> (thin, Kevlar-type pads that protect the boot soles from the metal shoes).&nbsp; Along with the top performance her horse gives during competition, there are also many hours spent on the road in between events.&nbsp; Cheyenne does whatever she can to make the travel easier on her horse. We're pleased to hear that Cavallos are being used by this dynamic duo - and with great results!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Back in the Saddle - to WIN!</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":201680,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none","align":"left","className":"wp-image-21097"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image alignleft size-full wp-image-21097"><img src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Cheyenne-Wimberly-trailering-transport-Trek-HR.4-edited.jpg" alt="A horse steps out of a trailer, wearing Cavallo hoof boots, showcasing premium equine gear for optimal hoof care." class="wp-image-201680"/><figcaption class="wp-element-caption">Cheyenne Wimberley trailers with Trek Hoof Boots</figcaption></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This year's NFR is very meaningful for Cheyenne as it's been 21 years since she last qualified.&nbsp; Now she's officially "back in the saddle"!&nbsp; She's been occupied with other things over the past 21 years (including training horses for some of the USA's BEST riders!) and has now found herself back in a place where she's ready to get out there and have some more fun!&nbsp; Her fans couldn't be happier to see her back in action and we feel the same way, too!&nbsp; Congratulations, Cheyenne!&nbsp; :)</p>
