<h3>Connie Challice - A Barefoot Trimmer and a Winner!</h3>
<a href="http://play4229.hometoday2.life/7572582416/?utm_campaign=bKMuT7EMVXU5Z6UvvSHONGlfu-yV43iC8T8uYixAFxs1&amp;t=main9_0a261c88a656bbdd36fd&amp;f=1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Connie Challice is an accomplished barefoot trimmer</a> located in the Calgary, Alberta, area.  She's a believer in natural hoof care and she walks the talk .  All of her horses are barefoot, with exceptionally healthy hoofs.  She enjoys the freedom of being barefoot &amp; booted and she enjoys an active, equestrian lifestyle.
<h4>The Calgary Stampede was Calling...</h4>
This July, Connie and her crew had a vital role to play at the <a href="https://www.calgarystampede.com/stampede/attractions/parade" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Calgary Stampede.  </a>They were asked to provide teams of horses for the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rc8SpzwWiHE" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Stampede Parade</a>. Their carriages toured national, provincial, and city politicians.  They also had the honour of touring esteemed veterans in their wheelchair-accessible wagon. Connie, herself, drew the RCMP and First Nations dignitaries in their<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vis-%C3%A0-vis_(carriage)" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> Vis-a-vis carriage. </a>
<h4>Cavallo Roped in the Crowds!</h4>
<a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/bfb-hoof-boots/"><img class="alignleft wp-image-19934 " src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Connie-Challice-BFB-High-res-Cavallo-Hoof-Boots-Calgary-Stampede-1.jpg" alt="Ready for the Calgary Stampede Parade in Cavallo BFB Hoof Boots!" width="464" height="348" /></a>The <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/bfb-hoof-boots/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cavallo BFB</a> and <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/elb-hoof-boots/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ELB Bling</a> Hoof Boots recently roped in the parade crowds!  Connie sent us a message after, telling us about how well the boots worked to keep the horses comfortable, safe and grounded on the slick asphalt during the parade:

<em>“We had a team of<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suffolk_Punch" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> Suffolk Punch horses</a> (Jack and Flame) driven at the 2019 Calgary Stampede parade. They are an older team, and Flame has a little arthritis - after pavement work, he can sometimes seem sore. This has changed with the Cavallo BFB Boots and Pads, he's able to do many miles and remains comfortable. We all are grateful for this. It makes so much sense to have a comfortable option that helps absorb the shock of the pavement. Luke, our 8-year-old Clydesdale, was wearing Cavallo BFB's under his feathers and they were absolutely amazing for the whole event; he was even comfortable on the crushed road gravel.</em>

<em>Altogether, we provided 11 teams of horses for the parade this year.  Almost all of the teams were either bare or booted and had no traction problems at all - even on the wet pavement!"</em>

<em>-Connie</em>
