<p>I’ve mentioned the following two associations in blogs before but here are their latest events, in case you are up for a new adventure!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Best of America By Horseback gang have two great rides coming up in October. One in the Leatherwood Mountains in North Carolina and then a beach ride in South Carolina!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Leatherwood ride includes horsemanship clinics, campfire cooking, cowboy music, and of course ride outs with Tom Seay each day. I would love to be doing this one! And fancy sauntering around a beach with 1000’s of other rider for a few days, and all in aid of charity? Then head to Myrtle Beach – this will be one to remember. <a href="http://www.bestofamericabyhorseback.com/upcomingridesevents.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">http://www.bestofamericabyhorseback.com/upcomingridesevents.html</a></p>

<p>Looking for something a bit closer to home? Well, the American Competitive Trail Horse Association has 56 events happening right across America in October alone! That’s’ right, 56 events! I can hardly believe it. So there is no excuse, find something near you and get out there and have fun! <a href="https://www.actha.us/events" target="_blank" rel="noopener">https://www.actha.us/events</a></p>
