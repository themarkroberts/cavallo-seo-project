<h3>A Flat-Footed Friend in Need...</h3>
Our older quarter horse has very flat feet. Prior to us getting him he had been metal-shod almost constantly, and had also been misdiagnosed with Laminitis. Using <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/elb-hoof-boots/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cavallo ELBs</a> on his front feet with <a href="http://cavallo.local/product/gel-pads/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Gel Pads</a> during the daytime (off at night) took him from limping around and barely moving to cantering around the field in about 1 day. No word of a lie … he was a different (and much happier) horse with his boots. We now only use the boots if the ground dries out a lot in the summer and for trail riding. But I would highly recommend them!
