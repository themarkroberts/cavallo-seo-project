<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Risks Associated with Transporting Horses and How Cavallo Hoof Boots Help</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Transporting horses can be stressful, both for the horse and the handler. Bumpy roads, tight turns, and long journeys can take a toll on your horse's hooves and overall comfort. Luckily, Cavallo Hoof Boots provide the protection needed for a smoother trip. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>These boots significantly increase shock absorption, which is especially important for bumpy roads or when extra support is needed on the highway. In addition, Cavallo Hoof Boots have scientifically proven soles that reduce pressure, absorb impact, and minimize stress on your horse's hooves and joints. This helps to keep them fresh and ready to perform upon arrival!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The boots provide added traction, reducing the chance of slipping during sharp turns or sudden stops. Additionally, during transport, horses may become stressed or impatient, leading to kicking. Cavallo Hoof Boots help soften the impact of these kicks, reducing the risk of injury to your horse or other horses in the trailer. In short, with Cavallo Hoof Boots, your horse can travel more safely, comfortably, and with less stress!</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Benefits of Cavallo Hoof Boots for Transport</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0"><br>Cavallo Hoof Boots provide exceptional protection and comfort during travel, ensuring your horse’s hooves stay safe and healthy. Their durable, shock-absorbing soles cushion hooves from impact, reducing bruising and stress during bumpy rides or long journeys. The scientifically designed sole helps reduce pressure on the hooves, providing added comfort even on rough roads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The breathable design of Cavallo Hoof Boots ensures hooves stay dry and comfortable, even during extended travel periods. Most importantly, this airflow helps to prevent excessive sweating and moisture buildup, reducing the risk of thrush or other hoof issues. Furthermore, with a secure fit that prevents slipping or shifting, Cavallo Hoof Boots give you peace of mind, knowing your horse’s hooves are protected without causing discomfort.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For horses that are traveling long distances or standing for extended periods, Cavallo Hoof Boots offer added joint support. The boots absorb shock and reduce strain, helping to relieve joint stiffness that may occur during long transport sessions. This feature makes Cavallo Hoof Boots especially beneficial for older horses or those prone to arthritis.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Optimizing Your Cavallo Hoof Boots for Transport</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Ensure Cavallo Hoof Boots fit snugly without being too tight or restrictive. A proper fit prevents shifting, rubbing, and discomfort during transport. Secure the Velcro straps evenly to keep the boots stable and supportive throughout the journey. If you need help with sizing, contact the Cavallo team for expert guidance.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Additionally, for long trips, enhance protection by adding Cavallo Pastern Wraps or Comfort Sleeves. These accessories cushion the pastern and heel bulbs, reducing pressure points and preventing irritation. For even greater comfort, insert hoof boot pads. Extra padding boosts shock absorption, minimizes hoof fatigue, and relieves strain on joints. This added cushioning benefits older horses and those with hoof sensitivities. Explore different padding options <a href="http://cavallo.local/faq/cavallo-hoof-boot-pads/">Here</a>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Once you arrive, continue using Cavallo Hoof Boots for extra support on pavement, showgrounds, or trails. The boots protect hooves from rough, uneven, or hard surfaces, keeping your horse comfortable in any environment. Whether you’re traveling for competitions, vet visits, or trail rides, Cavallo Hoof Boots provide lasting comfort and protection wherever you go.</p>
