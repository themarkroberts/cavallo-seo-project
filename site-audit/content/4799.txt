<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What On Earth Are These Girls Doing?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size">What YOU'VE Been Asking: Are Cavallo Boots Test Proven?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">If you’re interested in modern hoof care you likely follow us closely and know this already, BUT… If you’re new to booting, you may find yourself asking this question. We’ve had so many people asking by email, phone and message:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>“Are Cavallo Hoof Boots scientifically proven to benefit horses?”</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The answer is <strong>YES</strong>. In fact, we are the ONLY hoof boot company that can say our boots have been <a href="http://cavallo.local/cavallo-hoof-boots-scientific-hoof-boot-study-2/?utm_source=google-ads&amp;utm_campaign=illm221111permaxallhoofbootscoldpurld1&amp;gad_source=1&amp;gclid=CjwKCAiA_OetBhAtEiwAPTeQZ6upB6FMjt7LEyFeadnQNXPiP973TzFUqA--THKgaHNCZEm4SyLC4RoCAfwQAvD_BwE"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">scientifically tested</span></a>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Step into the groundbreaking study by Dr. Jennifer Gill and Gabriella Lynn from the <a href="https://equine.ca.uky.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Western Kentucky University Equine Sciences Department</span></a>. They concluded the first-ever independent university comparison between hoof boots and traditional metal shoes, with their top hoof boot pick being the remarkable <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/trek-hoof-boots/"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Cavallo Trek Hoof Boots</span></a>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Unlocking a realm of comfort, protection, and minimized hoof damage, the study is a testament to what <a href="http://cavallo.local/about/"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Carole</span></a> has been passionately sharing with the equine community since 2006.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The research delved into surface area, force, and pressure exerted on horse hooves navigating the unforgiving terrain of asphalt. Employing cutting-edge <a href="https://www.fujifilm.com/ca/en/business/industrial-materials/measurement-film/prescale" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">FujiFilm Prescale pressure measurement film</span></a>, the scientists gauged the pressure experienced by the hooves, revealing dynamic insights through vibrant color imaging.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>According to Dr. Gill, “The Cavallo Hoof Boots proved to be a game-changer, safeguarding the entire hoof and surpassing other forms of protection like traditional horseshoes. The thicker tread of the Cavallo Trek Hoof Boots provided superior ground contact compared to metal horseshoes.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The results were unequivocally in favour of Cavallo Trek Hoof Boots, as Dr. Gill explains, “The pressure recorded with Cavallo was significantly lower than other treatments, precisely as we anticipated. The increased surface area in contact with the ground, thanks to the substantial tread, offers horses better traction and enhanced ability to handle forces and pressure on challenging terrains.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo Hoof Boots FAQ!</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size">How Should I Store my Cavallo Boots?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It is best to store your Cavallo Boots in a cool, dry location with good air circulation. Boots that are wet should be allowed to dry before placing them in storage and should NOT be stored in the original box.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As with most hoof boot brands, Cavallo soles are moulded in industrial-grade TPU (Thermoplastic Urethane). Under certain conditions, this compound may be vulnerable to a chemical reaction that can happen when older boots are stored (not used) for prolonged periods, in unfavourable conditions.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This can be avoided by ensuring good air ventilation and avoiding humidity over 70%. A combination of temperatures as high as 40 Degrees Celsius with high moisture content should be avoided. &nbsp;Follow these easy instructions and your hoof boots will last for many years.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">Please be wary if you are purchasing your boots second-hand, as you may not have information on how the boots were stored or cared for prior to you owning them.</p>

<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
