<p>Horse trainer John Lyons, has long been called “America’s Most Trusted Horseman”. He’s been presenting training clinics and horsemanship symposia since 1980. HJohn has also written several books and is the founder of John Lyons’ Perfect Horse magazine. His son, Josh Lyons, has now furthered his father’s techniques and spends his days at horse training clinics, giving private instruction, and teaching at his equine events.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>John is currently active in the John and Josh Lyons Certification program and the Josh Lyons Accreditation program.</p>
