<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="color: #000000;">There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven</span></h3>

<!-- wp:image {"id":11778,"align":"left"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image alignleft"><img src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/book-1-300x300-2.jpg" alt="Carole Herder's Book There are no Horseshoes in Heaven" class="wp-image-11778"/></figure>

<p><span style="color: #000000;">Effectively manage your horse’s well-being naturally. There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven offers a profound and passionate exploration of past patterns, guiding you through an entertaining and thought-provoking invitation to make alternative choices. Protect your horse from pain and discomfort, maximize your riding experience and gain insight into the ‘hot spots’ for horses, through scientific evidence and real-life stories. There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven exposes the passé ways of thinking that have impacted our horses. This book examines horses’ natural way of being and shows how small changes in our husbandry can yield big results. It calls us to apply the philosophy of empowerment with a healthy dose of curiosity and move forward into interesting new times.</span></p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="color: #000000;">Cavallo Barefoot Trim Guide</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":67488,"align":"left"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image alignleft"><img src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Cavallo-Barefoot-Trim-1-194x300-3.jpg" alt="Cavallo Barefoot Trim Guide" class="wp-image-67488"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">From age 10, Lynn Seeley was shoeing horses. He developed a great respect, sensitivity and understanding for all breeds and their respective purpose and way of going. Most horses had the common assortment of problems. Reconstructions, resections, treating founder and laminitis were common practice. He understood how the hoof affects the entire horse and went on to improve their well-being through removing metal shoes and trimming barefoot horses to health and soundness. Now with over 60 years of practice, he shares his gentle trimming method through this illustrated manual and accompanying video hosted by Herder. This manual should be in every horse woman and man’s library either as reference for yourself or as guidance for your trimmer.</span></p>
