<p><a href="http://cavallo.local" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider</a> is located on the spectacular <a href="https://sunshinecoastcanada.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Sunshine Coast</a> in BC, Canada.&nbsp; It’s a small town (that’s quickly becoming a large one!). Our offices are here, in amongst the trees and pastures.&nbsp; We – and our animals, feel very fortunate to live here.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo Worldwide</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>At the present, <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cavallo Hoof Boots</a> are sold in more than 26 countries Worldwide.&nbsp; Here, in this little town, when I am asked what I ‘do’, people are surprised when I tell them that my company, Cavallo, is a ‘hoof boot’ company.&nbsp; You mean “hoof” like horses? Yes -&nbsp; and I must then explain the function of the hoof, and why hoof boots are such a popular choice over metal shoes among horse owners.&nbsp; It’s always interesting to watch people’s faces as they process this information.&nbsp; “Wow!&nbsp; What a great idea! Why hasn’t anyone done this before?”&nbsp; It’s the pain free thing.&nbsp; And our inherent love for horses.&nbsp; Whether people have ridden or not or even been around horses, they see horses as the freedom, nature and wildness that we innately long for.&nbsp; We just want to see horses living naturally, pain free.&nbsp; And they get it.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo - Locally!</h4>

<p>Recently, our local newspaper, <a href="http://www.coastreporter.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">The Coast Reporter</a>, printed an article about Cavallo.&nbsp; As you likely have seen, we had a <a href="https://www.facebook.com/emmamassingale/videos/1794099077554732/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">social media post about Cavallo go viral</a> at Christmas that got us a LOT of worldwide attention!&nbsp; This video post was produced by our favourite Female Equine Adventurer, <a href="http://www.emmamassingale.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Emma Massingale.</a> Our local paper felt that our community would be interested to know that we are here in <a href="https://sunshinecoastcanada.com/explore/south-coast/roberts-creek/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Roberts Creek</a>, tucked away, doing a VERY unique thing.&nbsp; We were completely bowled over by the local response to the article!&nbsp; Everywhere I go now, I have people coming up to me saying “So THAT’S what you do!&nbsp; That is SO COOL!”.&nbsp; People are fascinated.&nbsp; It has been a lot of fun for us, and we see it as one more small step toward educating the public about the needs of horses.</p>
