<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Ground-Breaking Research by Barefoot Horse Researcher, Sarah Albanozzo</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-bottom:27px">Sarah Albanozzo is a Cavallo Hoof Boot lover and a passionate believer in barefoot and booted lifestyle for optimal horse health.&nbsp;<br>She is a Research Project Manager at the University of Malta and a part-time student at the Royal (Dick) School of Veterinary Studies at the University of Edinburgh. Sarah is also an independent researcher on equine hoof morphology and has been a devout equestrian for 42 years.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">Sarah has shown us that the hooves of barefoot-kept horses are capable of walking over hard ground due to their physiology. Since the results showed accelerated sole growth and since there is no information documented in equine veterinary literature, she compared the horse’s foot to the human foot, focusing on a particular mechanoreceptor that is responsible for promoting sole growth. The success of this study, however, was also due to frequent non-invasive trimming cycles (keeping hoof walls level with the sole and toes short) and the fact that the animals were on a 100% forage-based diet with 24/7 turnout, roaming over varied terrain. When comparing locomotion in barefoot and booted horses versus shod horses, the difference is very evident, as noted in the published study below.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The peer-reviewed and published paper, Hoof Capability of Barefoot-Kept Horses and Ponies Walking over Artificial Environments: An Anatomical and Radiological Study, is a joint collaboration with Prof. Robert M Bowker (VMD PhD), Dr Louis Borg (DVM), and Prof. Liberato Camilleri. We’re so proud to share Sarah’s important study with YOU, our Cavallo community.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<p style="padding-right:0;padding-left:0">President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
