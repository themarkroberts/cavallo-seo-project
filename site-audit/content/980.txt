<strong><em>Cavallo’s  Carole Herder and Carrie Scrima of The</em></strong><strong> <em>American Competitive Trail Horse Association </em></strong>(ACTHA) chat candidly about the evolution in Cavallo Hoof Boots, the fast paced changes in an otherwise traditional industry and how it all coincides to create an innovative healthy option for your horse’s comfort and well being. The setting is not too bad as they converse on the river bank deep in the heart of Texas.

Traveling from the Entrepreneur Awards in Toronto en route to a conference in Costa Rica, Carole side tracked over to meet with Carrie.

“It’s really time to embrace the changes and put the power back in the hands of us girls – the horse owners.” Carole enthused. “Partnering with ACTHA helps get the word out. Ladies - pull off those metal shoes and use Cavallos to ensure your horse is comfortable and protected. And then get out and RIDE!”

Cavallo is proud to be the exclusive Hoof Boot of ACTHA providing the infrastructure for competitive trail challenges that are hosted by affiliates around the country. These challenges blend trail riding and versatility. Although ACTHA is not a non-profit organization, up to 50% of its proceeds are donated to horse charities and rescues.
