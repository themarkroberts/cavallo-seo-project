We just got done from a 5 mile ride in the new snazzy red simple boots. This is the first time Stormy has ever worn hoof boots. Stormy loved them and moved out beautifully. Although he is 100% sound barefoot, there is too much dangerous debris on the roads and I know his feet are safer in the boots. We got a lot of great comments from onlookers on his sharp looking red running shoes. I always ask them how comfortable would they be in steel running shoes? The same goes for horses! Protection and shock absorption. Stormy is 20 years old and performs like a 5 year old. No joint problems or arthritis at all because he has been barefoot for the last 9 years!
