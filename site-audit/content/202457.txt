<h2 class="wp-block-heading">???? BREAKING NEWS from Spoga Horse! ????</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size">Cavallo Hoof Boots and Consteed have just made history!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-bottom:27px">We are beyond thrilled to announce that Greg Giles, CEO of Cavallo and Henner Horschen of Continental, have been awarded the prestigious TOP INNOVATIONS 2025 award at Spoga Horse, selected from 31 outstanding nominees! They have won this prestigious award for the co-branded Consteed smart hoof sneaker!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Spoga Horse in Germany is the leading innovation trade fair of the international equestrian industry. The Spoga Top Innovations Award celebrates a groundbreaking leap in equine care—and Consteed is the first and only diagnostic hoof boot capable of reporting and predicting lameness. Combining horse well-being, artificial intelligence, and world-class manufacturing, this revolutionary innovation sets a new gold standard in hoof health.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>With decades of expertise, Cavallo and Consteed, two esteemed industry leaders, have united to redefine what’s possible in equine technology. The future of preventative hoof care and performance monitoring is here!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">A huge thank you to Spoga Horse for recognizing this innovation, and huge congratulations to all the nominees, including AJR SPORT, Ariat Europe, Horseware Ireland, LeMieux Ltd, and more.</p>
