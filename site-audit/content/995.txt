When CEO, Greg Giles first joined the Cavallo team, he had never been on a horse before. Now he and Carole ride everywhere they get a chance.  He has become like the Man from Snowy River through a very unlikely background. Greg’s history is truly corporate. He was the 28 Year Founding Managing Director of Mondopoint  - an Australian based International footwear trading company, 7 years as Marketing Director for Geelong Football Club, Director of Geelong Tourism, Director of the Geelong Economic Development Unit and Chairman of Geelong Major Events.

This successful, intelligent and infinitely joyful Australian has now turned his attention to booting horses and we in the horse world are indeed grateful. Thank you to Greg for all you do in making horses all over the planet more comfortable with Cavallo hoof boots.
