<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Navicular Syndrome: Often Misunderstood</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Navicular Disease, also known as Navicular Syndrome or Caudal Heel Pain, is a frequently diagnosed yet often misunderstood condition. Its symptoms emerge gradually, manifesting in subtle ways: pinned ears, tail swishing, reluctance to pick up the correct lead, an uneasy stance, or even tripping on uneven ground. Your horse may seem slightly off, but pinpointing the cause can be difficult. Signs such as shifting weight, an unwillingness to stand square, or reluctance to lift a hoof for the farrier should not be overlooked—they are early warnings of a deeper issue.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">A Natural Solution for Hoof Pain &amp; Injury</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Conventional treatments often involve stall rest, modified exercise routines, anti-inflammatory medications, or bar shoes. While these methods may provide temporary relief, they do not address the root cause: restricted circulation and impaired hoof function. The best way to restore soundness and prevent further degeneration is to encourage natural movement and proper blood flow—which is exactly what Cavallo Hoof Boots are designed to do.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Why Cavallo Hoof Boots?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots are more than just protection; they are an integral part of your horse’s healing process. Designed to support optimal hoof function, Cavallo Boots allow the hoof to expand, flex, and absorb shock naturally, reducing strain on tendons and ligaments. By facilitating unrestricted blood circulation, they promote healing and prevent further deterioration.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Beyond Navicular Syndrome, Cavallo Hoof Boots provide therapeutic relief for a variety of hoof ailments, including abscesses, laminitis, sole bruising, injuries, and overall hoof sensitivity.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"24px","bottom":"32px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:32px">Whether your horse is in recovery or in need of ongoing hoof protection, Cavallo Boots offer a practical and reliable solution.</p>
