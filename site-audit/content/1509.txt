<p>Cavallo provides ultimate protection for the barefoot horse.&nbsp; All Cavallo Hoof Boots styles are excellent for comfort, as well as for relief from chronic pain or hoof sensitivity; rehabilitation from injuries, abscesses, navicular disease, laminitis / founder, punctures, sole bruising and contracted heels.&nbsp; They're also great for protection during trailering and breeding, or taken along on the trail as a spare tire for lost shoes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>All Cavallo Boots are simple to put on and take off - and being shaped for the natural hoof, stay securely in place.&nbsp; The molded-in back flap protects the bulb and provides abrasion resistance.&nbsp; Durably constructed Cavallo boots have built-in drainage, are made with waterproof thread, rustproof metal, and a high-performance TPU outsole.&nbsp; These lightweight boots absorb shock so your horse's legs don't have to!&nbsp; You'll love Cavallos for their ability to promote healthy hooves by allowing the expansion and contraction necessary to increase blood circulation.</p>
