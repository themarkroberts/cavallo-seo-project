<h2 class="wp-block-heading">How the Dans Chose the RIGHT Hoof Boot:</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Many years ago, the Dans were searching for a hoof boot that they could take on the road to trainer’s shows and challenges. They knew they needed a boot that would make their horses feel confident and secure on carpet, indoor floors concrete and other challenging surfaces.&nbsp; Well, they found those boots – and they’ve been using&nbsp;<a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/trek-hoof-boots/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Cavallo Trek Boots</a>&nbsp;ever since!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"placeholder":"Content…"} -->
<p>It’s no secret that&nbsp;The Double Dans (Double Dan Horsemanship)&nbsp;have always loved their&nbsp;<a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Cavallo Hoof Boots.&nbsp;</a>&nbsp;Like Cavallo, Double Dan Horsemanship was developed for the love of the horse. Partners&nbsp;Dan James&nbsp;and&nbsp;Dan Steers&nbsp;work towards helping to create well-rounded horses in any discipline that become perfect equine partners.
