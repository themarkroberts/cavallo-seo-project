<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Emma Massingale - Tricycle Trip for 3 through Europe!&nbsp; Europe - one Smile at a Time!</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Emma is on to her next big adventure - and this one is going to require a lot of leg work - literally!&nbsp; Here's what Emma is saying about her upcoming journey:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter"><img src="https://i1.wp.com/www.emmamassingale.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/COver-Pic5619resampl.jpg?resize=1024%2C731&amp;ssl=1" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>"Three years of full-time adventure and ten years of creating media content, I have learnt that one of the most rewarding things is to be able to make other people smile! If we can also back this up with some fun horsemanship and an epic adventure then it’s something worth getting pretty excited about!</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>I knew this year I wanted to do an adventure that included my miniatures…but where to start as they are too small to ride and have very short legs, not ideal for travelling long distances!&nbsp; I knew it had to be something personally challenging for me but fun and easy for the ponies.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Throughout history we have needed horses to pull or carry us but what now if in the 21st Century these little horses could have one simple role, to make people smile! My horses make me smile everyday, this is not only incredible therapy but has helped me become the horsewomen I am today. Horses recognize&nbsp;facial expressions, I have said for many years that as crazy as it sounds just smiling at your horse can help form greater friendships and if they like us more they are going to perform better for us in any situation.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>I am going to take my two untrained miniatures, one Miniature Horse (Percy – Appaloosa) and one Miniature Shetland Pony (Stanley) 1000 miles across Europe on an epic adventure. I shall be cycling along with my minis in a purpose built tricycle across 6 countries through Europe with two main objectives in mind, to give everyone we meet and those may like to follow us online their daily smile…(it’s impossible not to!) and to have an epic adventure that challenges me both physically and mentally as I can assure you that cycling along with two horses on board isn’t easy! Stanley, Percy and I shall camp each night under the stars."</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>GO EMMA!</p>
