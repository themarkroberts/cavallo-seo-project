At this year’s Amerequine  Expo at the Will Rogers Memorial Center in Fort Worth, Texas, President Carole Herder met with one of our good friends, Cyndy Branham of R&amp;D Ranch. Cyndy is a certified Trainer/Clinician and is well known for her participation in the Extreme Mustang Makeover and ACTHA’s Favorite Trail Horse Competitions. She was Top female competitor and finalist at the Extreme Mustang Makeover 2008, Reserve Champion at the Mustang Magic 2009 and  Finalist at the Supreme Extreme Mustang Makeover in 2010.  Cyndy shares in our belief that alleviating any discomfort in horses is an integral part of training and ultimately having a successful relationship with your horses and to that end highly praises Cavallo products!
