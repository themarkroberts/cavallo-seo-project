<h3><strong>Just Giving Awards - Cavallo Worldwide Ambassador Nominated!</strong></h3>
Cavallo is extremely proud that our Worldwide Ambassador, <a href="https://www.emmamassingale.com/about" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Emma Massingale</a>, is up for a prestigious <a href="https://pages.contact.justgiving.com/JustGivingVoting2019/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Just Giving Award!</a>  Many who follow Emma on her <a href="https://www.emmamassingale.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">website,</a> <a href="https://www.facebook.com/emmamassingale" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Facebook,</a> or <a href="https://www.instagram.com/emmassingale/?hl=en" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Instagram</a> may already feel like they can count her as a friend.  This is due to her kind, magnetic personality that shines through in her videos and photographs. When working with her talented herd at liberty (horses, ponies, minis, donkeys - even the occasional turkey!), they gather around her in perfect trust and willingness.  I'm hoping that all of you in the equine community will cast a vote in Emma's direction. She truly deserves to be honoured with this award.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4oiyEkHd-8M

Just Giving is one of the largest platforms for online fundraising. Emma's been nominated for their 2019 Creative Fundraiser of the Year Award.  Through Just Giving, Emma’s recent journey across Europe (towing her two mini horses and her dog along in a custom-built tricycle) managed to raise £2,331.91!  This money went straight to Brooke Action for Working Horses and Donkeys.
<h3><strong>Hear it from Emma:</strong></h3>
<em>"I'm so excited to announce that our cycling across Europe adventure is a finalist in this years national Just Giving awards in the “Creative Fundraiser of the year” category!</em>

<em>While Stanley and Percy have very easy jobs, many horses around the world aren’t that lucky. I chose to use this trip to support<a href="https://www.thebrooke.org/about-brooke/brooke-glance?gclid=EAIaIQobChMI5cat_Nb24wIVgq_sCh2gzQK7EAAYASAAEgIqv_D_BwE" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> the Brooke Foundation.</a>  Brooke raises money and awareness for working horses and donkeys around the world. Horses have pulled us in carts and wagons for centuries. It seemed appropriate to use the uniqueness of my adventure to help this amazing charity!</em>

<em>Stan, Percy, Inca and I cycled across Europe in a purpose-built tricycle.  We rode through Switzerland, France and Germany, finishing in the Netherlands 1034 km later!  The video above shows a few highlights from our trip.</em>
