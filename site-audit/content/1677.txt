<p>Cavallo Community was out in full force at the <a href="https://chilliwack.maneeventexpo.com/">2017 Mane Event in </a><a href="https://chilliwack.maneeventexpo.com/">Chilliwack, </a><a href="https://chilliwack.maneeventexpo.com/">British Columbia! </a>&nbsp;One of the many stars who visited our booth was Darcie&nbsp;Kerkhoven, confirmed barefoot Champion 3- day Eventer, teacher and coach.&nbsp; Catherine Davidson of <a href="http://www.bchorsemen.org/bchbc/">BCH-BC (Back Country Horsemen of British Columbia)</a> came by with some of her members to&nbsp;do a little shopping.&nbsp; We love the BCH-BC. These are the people who really get out and ride those trails.&nbsp; If you are a member or are considering it: through Cavallo sponsorship you can get a pretty sweet deal.&nbsp; Jenny was thrilled because she got to put faces to names of all those folks she chats with by phone and email.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Our favorite Vet, <a href="http://geertsema.ca/about/">Hermen Geertsema</a> took some time to visit. Let me tell you, he trims a mean hoof when he has to! Unfortunately, of course we pay vet fees for this service.&nbsp; LOL.</p>
