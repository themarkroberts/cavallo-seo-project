<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Simple Boots Shine in Ella Langley’s AE Ad</h3>

<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">Wait… did you catch that?! ? Yep - that's <a href="https://www.grammy.com/news/ella-langley-hungover-new-album-interview" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Ella Langley!</a></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A customer recently shared <a href="https://www.youtube.com/shorts/p6-PFgOolTM?feature=share" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">the new American Eagle video featuring Ella Langley,</a> and there’s a detail that’s hard to miss: the horse she’s riding is wearing <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/simple-hoof-boots/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Cavallo Hoof Boots.</a> Seeing those boots appear in a major fashion campaign is a pretty exciting moment.</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It’s something to feel proud of: the same boots trusted on your horses are also chosen for big productions. From Beyoncé’s <a href="https://horse-canada.com/horse-news/beyonces-horse-sports-cavallo-booties/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">“Beyoncé Bowl” NFL Halftime performance</a> to a mini horse in CLBs at the Oscars, professional horses in show business rely on Cavallos for safety and protection—and that speaks volumes.</p>

<div class="wp-block-column is-vertically-aligned-top" style="padding-top:0;padding-right:0;padding-bottom:0;padding-left:0"><!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}},"elements":{"link":{"color":{"text":"var:preset|color|primary"}}}},"textColor":"primary"} -->
<p class="has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color" style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">The horse world has lost a truly remarkable soul with the passing of Endo the Blind. Our hearts go out to Morgan Wagner and everyone who loved and cared for this extraordinary horse.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"elements":{"link":{"color":{"text":"var:preset|color|primary"}}}},"textColor":"primary"} -->
<p class="has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color">Endo’s story touched people far beyond the equestrian community. After losing both of his eyes to disease, his owner was faced with a difficult choice. With patience, trust, and an incredible partnership with Morgan, Endo went on to live a life that inspired millions worldwide. Endo not only continued to ride, train, and perform - he <a href="https://www.guinnessworldrecords.com/news/2022/12/endo-the-blind-horse-without-eyes-achieves-three-records-728008" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">achieved three Guinness World Records,</a> proving to the world that blindness does not define ability.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"elements":{"link":{"color":{"text":"var:preset|color|primary"}}}},"textColor":"primary"} -->
<p class="has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color">I had the honour and pleasure of meeting the beautiful Endo at Western States Horse Expo last year. This boy showed us the power of trust between horse and human. He reminded us that horses experience the world through so much more than sight. And he demonstrated, beautifully and bravely, that limitations often exist only in our assumptions.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"elements":{"link":{"color":{"text":"var:preset|color|primary"}}}},"textColor":"primary"} -->
<p class="has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color">Endo’s legacy will live on in every horse owner who believes a little more in what their horse can do, and in every horse who is given a chance because someone remembered his story.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"elements":{"link":{"color":{"text":"var:preset|color|primary"}}}},"textColor":"primary"} -->
<p class="has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color">From all of us here, we send our deepest condolences to Morgan and to everyone whose lives were touched by this magnificent horse.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"elements":{"link":{"color":{"text":"var:preset|color|primary"}}}},"textColor":"primary"} -->
<p class="has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color">Run free, Endo. The world was brighter because of you.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
