<h3 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">What the Cavallo Hoof Boots Donations and Sponsorship Program Offers</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading --></div>
<!-- /wp:group -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p class="has-text-align-center">Aimed at offering the equine community assistance and support while helping conscious equestrians, Cavallo contributes to organizations such as, but not exclusive to, horse rescues, therapy centres, child literacy groups, youth equestrian groups and more.<br><br>Through targeted partnerships, Cavallo assists equine organizations by providing donations in the form of prizes, auction items, gift coupons, and free products. Cavallo Hoof Boots helps organizations raise the funds needed to continue their important work.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The Cavallo Hoof Boots Influencer Program</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"24px","bottom":"32px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:32px">The Cavallo Hoof Boots Influencer Program is a dynamic initiative designed for collaboration with passionate equestrians and influencers who share a commitment to the best in high-quality hoof care. Cavallo influencers hold their horse’s health and comfort in the highest regard. Influencer activities range from trail riders to dressage riders, equine podiatrists to veterinarians and farriers, and include barrel racers, showjumpers and Olympians!<br><br>Partnering with key figures in the equestrian community provides a showcase of the benefits and innovative features of Cavallo hoof boots. Influencers play a crucial role in promoting and testing Cavallo products, offering their expertise and knowledge to a broader audience. Their authentic and engaging content educates and highlights the ease of use, quality performance and extensive health benefits of barefoot and booting, ultimately contributing to the well-being of horses worldwide.</p>
