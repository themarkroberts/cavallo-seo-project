<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">Step Into the Future with the World's Most Intelligent Hoof Boot!</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns"><!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We are so excited to be in the final design stages of the world’s first smart boot. This is truly a game changer, which will allow you to understand your horse’s health and well-being in real time through AI.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Imagine viewing your horse’s gait, balance, ground force reaction and more on your own personal device. This diagnostic tool can identify early signs of lameness before they are even visible to the eye. The sensors are placed in the sole of Cavallo Hoof Boots, transforming it into the world’s first diagnostic hoof boot.</p>

<p>Underneath this mask is another Cavallo Boot lover!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Texas Masked Rider is the well-known and beloved official mascot at Texas Tech University in Texas. A student selected through a competitive process holds the sought-after position of Masked Rider. A symbol of school spirit and tradition, the Masked Rider embodies the university’s heritage and values. The role is steeped in tradition and is meant to inspire pride and enthusiasm among students, alumni, and fans.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Masked Rider leads the Texas Tech Red Raiders football team onto the field at home games and is a prominent figure during football games, riding a horse and energizing the crowd. The famous saying, “Guns Up!” is a spirited rally cry and signature exclamation that embodies the energy and enthusiasm of the Texas Tech University spirit.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Texas Tech Masked Rider has exclusively used Cavallo Hoof Boots for many years. Cavallos are known for their excellent traction and not coming off the hoof, making them a good choice for the high-activity demands of the Masked Rider’s performances.&nbsp;</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">But How Do they ALWAYS Stay ON?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns"><!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column"><!-- wp:image {"id":327833,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/image-1-2.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-327833"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image --></div>
<!-- /wp:column -->

<!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Hoof Boots tackle the issue of fit by mimicking the natural shape of your horse’s hooves. ⁠<br>⁠<br>Their conical shape, secured with industrial-strength Velcro, ensures that your boots stay put when properly sized and fitted. ⁠<br>⁠<br>Cavallo Boots are valued for protecting the entire hoof, including the bulb area. They have a tongue that adjusts to your horse’s hoof angle and a soft, padded leather collar for added security. ⁠<br>⁠<br>Inside, leather-covered foam padding shapes to your horse’s bulb area during the break in period, offering a snug, custom fit. This unique design PLUS the option of a Regular (roundish) or Slim (more narrow) sole guarantees your Cavallos stay on, no matter the hoof type or terrain. Reach out to us today and find a boot that fits your every need!</p>

<p class="has-large-font-size" style="margin-top:0;margin-bottom:0">Carole Herder</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"padding":{"right":"0","left":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="padding-right:0;padding-left:0">President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
