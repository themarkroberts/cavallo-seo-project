Check out Lorna in Scotland TREC rider for Team Great Britain.  Cavallo - keeping horses safe and comfortable all around the world.

The Cavallo Hoof boots are marvelous.  They demonstrated their all terrain capability in July when I found myself leading a trail ride for a friend. Having just trimmed Paebals feet her old boots were too slack and she pulled them off each time we cantered.  It was extremely frustrating.  As soon as possible I swapped to the Cavallo Sport Hoof Boots I never looked back (or down) for the rest of the week.
