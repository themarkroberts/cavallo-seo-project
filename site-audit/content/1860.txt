<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Hard Working Horse Lovers!</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The <a href="https://www.ponyclub.org/Certifications/NationalLevel/Certifications.aspx" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">H-HM/H/H-A Horse Management Certification</a> requires knowledge, experience, and maturity. Certified members must evaluate and care for horses’ needs efficiently in a variety of circumstances. Certified members must competently ground train horses and teach riding and horse care to others. The certification follows a rigorous study of theory, as well as learning the practical aspects of unmounted horse management. This certification-earning education is comparable to that of a college-level curriculum. Yep - these folks have worked HARD!</p>

<p>We fully recognize the hard work and dedication it takes to achieve H-HM/H/H-A Level Horse Management Certification. We also know it takes an unwavering commitment to the health and well-being of horses to reach this level.</p>
