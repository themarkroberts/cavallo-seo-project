<h3 class="wp-block-heading">What Are Cookies?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cookies are small text files stored on your web browser by a website you visit. These files allow websites to recognize you during future visits and improve your browsing experience. Cookies can be classified as “persistent” (stored until deleted) or “session” (deleted when the browser is closed).</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->

<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Provide analytics to improve user experience.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Store user preferences.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Deliver advertisements, including behavioral advertising.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
