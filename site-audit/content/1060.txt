<p>Combining the accuracy of show jumping with the thrill of cross country eventing, Jumpcross is the UK's fastest growing equestrian sport. Cavallo's UK Distributor, Battles, put on an amazing Jumpcross event last week, inviting their prized top retailers for a day of impeccable hospitality and entertainment. Fifty-one International riders competed for US$12,000.00 in prize money. Cavallo CEO, Greg Giles enjoyed the day mingling with the competitors and meeting a number of key Cavallo UK retailers, consistently full of praise for Cavallo products.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Amongst the field of elite riders was Oliver Townend winner of both Badminton AND Burghley in 2009. WOW! Oliver was very interested in Cavallo Hoof Boots - a product he assumed had perfect application for use in case of a 'lost shoe'. And became even more fascinated when Greg explained that Cavallo hoof boots are ideal for transportation. Protecting prized horses is a top priority and by offering 100% hoof protection especially for the quarters and coronet, Cavallo boots are a must have. Oliver is eagerly awaiting delivery of his Cavallo Boots to transport all his horses!</p>
