<h2><span style="color: #000000;">Cavallo Hoof Boots Q&amp;A: Important Points to Consider when Buying Hoof Boots</span></h2>
<span style="color: #000000;">As a designer/creator of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hoof_boot" target="_blank" rel="noopener">horse hoof boots</a>, I<strong> </strong>understand that buying hoof boots can be a daunting task. There's a lot to learn, but learning about hoof boots is time well spent. The question below made me think deeply about the process of buying boots and what the most important features are to consider for your horse:</span>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong><em>Q:</em></strong><strong> <em>Carole, there are now so many boot brands on the market that I get overwhelmed when doing my research. Do you have any solid pointers I can follow closely to make sure I get a boot that will serve my horse well?</em></strong></span></h3>
<span style="color: #000000;"><em><strong>A: While there are many reasons to boot your horse, you have an important decision to make – which hoof boots should you purchase? </strong> You can look to social media, product reviews, or online forums to determine the value of any purchase you might be considering, but how do you know which source of information to trust?</em></span>

<span style="color: #000000;"><em>Since the selection you make will greatly impact your horse’s performance, it’s best not to leave it up to chance. Instead, make a well-informed decision using the following checklist of considerations!  This checklist will help you make the right decision when shopping for quality hoof boots for your equine companion.</em></span>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>1. Value</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Well made, long-lasting, and durable.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Proven and established by years of rigorous use and many happy customers worldwide. As booting gains popularity, new brands are popping up vying for your purchase. Many are a variation of “a better mousetrap.” Confirmation of such things as quality, delivery, and warranties must be demonstrated.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>2. </strong><strong>Ease of use</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Easy to put on and take off – This saves time and is ideal for arthritic hands and young riders.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Simple design: Horses are rough on their footwear. Look for a brand with no unnecessary parts that will break down or gadgets that may need to be replaced, fixed, or adjusted.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>3. Safety</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">100% hoof protection over vulnerable areas such as bulbs, quarters, and coronet bands. Sensitive tissues should not be exposed as they are easily injured.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Front-opening systems: These keep you in front of the horse when putting on the boots rather than behind or beside.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Provide excellent traction.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Can be used with studs on slippery frozen ground and wet grass.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong><img class=" wp-image-62763 alignleft" src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Evelyn-Santer-Alps-Black-Trek-cute-dog-1-1.jpg" alt="Black Cavallo Trek Horse Hoof Boots" width="392" height="392" />4. Purpose</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">The best boots offer a versatile range of use. Look for boots that can be used for treatment and therapy, during turnout and travel, with injuries, for training, on any terrain, and at any speed.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Appropriate for all disciplines.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>5. Function</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Look for boots that don’t require tools or extra effort to use or maintain. When you barely have enough time to ride, the last thing you need is to be fidgeting with tools!</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Watch out for restrictive straps, hard plastic pieces, or any fastenings that can cause pressure and injury.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Drainage and aeration are essential; however, if the boot is too open, rocks and debris could enter and cause harm.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Boot upper should be flexible – Anything too rigid can cause undue pressure and interference.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Boots should allow for riding through any terrain at any speed.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3></h3>
<h3></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>6. Price</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Look for a reasonable price for lifespan and longevity – In many cases, you get what you pay for, so invest in quality!</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>7. Fit</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Adjustable throughout the trim cycle – You should be able to fasten boots differently during the growth cycle to maintain a snug, secure fit.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Adjustable throughout yearly hoof growth changes.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Compatible with different trims.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Styles should be offered in both Slim (narrow) and Regular (rounder) soles to give the best possible fit.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Able to fit over metal shoes as well as a bare hoof.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>8. Options</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Drainage holes that can be easily modified to use the boot as a soaking/therapy boot</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Changeable straps and buckle options for different terrain and conditions; examples include buckles for spear grass or snowpack and reflective straps for safety.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Colour selections are always nice.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>9. Care and maintenance</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Trouble-free – no maintenance besides hosing off with water.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>10. Comfort</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">The sole design prevents snowballing.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Comfortable sole that provides protection from rocks and aggressive surfaces</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Well-cushioned, appropriately thick sole that offers comfort on both dry, hard summer ground and frozen winter ground, as well as on rocks and aggressive or jagged terrain.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">A versatile array of hoof boot insole pads for every purpose, including therapeutic, comfort, travel, sole protection from metal shoes, etc.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Pastern wraps and sleeves for bulb and pastern protection or to adjust fit.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Must be soft or padded to cushion tender bulb area and protect from over-reaching, pressure, or other injuries.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Sole is designed for maximum shock absorption.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>11. Service</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">The company should offer superior, individual, and personal customer service and support from like-minded animal lovers.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>12. Evidence</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Best boot warranty for length of time and replacement or repair. Be sure to read the fine print.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">Social proof – Should have excellent and multiple user reviews.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="color: #000000;">It’s a bonus if the product is verified by a scientific hoof boot study.</span></li>
</ul>
