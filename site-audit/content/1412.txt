<p>I didn’t have to dig deep when asked to contribute a story for Horse Tales. Looking back, I have certainly experienced my share of hilarious, embarrassing, entertaining and even unnerving incidents as a horse person. It was fun to reflect, but difficult to choose only one. My story is called “You Are no Horse Trader” and because we at Cavallo are passionate about moving beyond preconceived notions and tradition, it seemed appropriate to share a funny story about my own personal naiveté &nbsp;and narrow mindedness.</p>
