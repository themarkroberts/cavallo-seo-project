Every year Carole and Greg look forward to the pleasure of hosting our fabulous customers Valley Vet Supply in our own private dining room at The Brown Palace in Denver. The Independence Room is wallpapered with prints from wood blocks created in Alsace-Lorraine in 1834 and is so beautiful it was also chosen to hang in the Diplomatic Receiving Room of the White House. This room used to be an entrance to the hotel, but was closed off and renovated into a private room due to the havoc and disturbance created by the infamous rumoured ghosts of the Brown Palace.

We chat, drink wine, give gifts and talk about our partnership.

This year was a fabulous 2010 and we had much to celebrate. What FUN!
