<h2 class="wp-block-heading">A Love Story that Only Rachel Can Tell…</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>From Rachel:</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Here’s the full story of my dreamy, horsey, engagement. The one thing Brian wanted to achieve with this proposal was the element of surprise. If you know us, you know how challenging that is! Not only do we live together, but we live in the tiniest of tiny homes, so hiding anything is difficult.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The biggest roadblock I expected was the cost of an engagement ring (though I have told Brian in the past that I really don’t care about the ring). I had NO idea my grandmother’s ring had been sitting in my sister’s house for the past several weeks, waiting for this exact moment!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"placeholder":"Content…"} -->
<p>Brian wanted to ensure the most magical experience possible while also surprising me. So, not only did he carefully guide me towards this beach adventure without me growing suspicious, but he also created a backup surprise! A very special video… Read on!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></div>
<!-- /wp:column --></div>
<!-- /wp:columns -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns"><!-- wp:column -->
<div class="wp-block-column"><!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Proposal Co-Conspirators Unveiled!</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>After we arrived, Tucker and I rode the length of the beach and back, and Brian asked me to come over for a photo. Once I got there, he started talking about how long we’d been together, and I was SO confused. Then he kneeled, and I laughingly said, “NOOO, stop that!” and truly thought he was joking. But then he pulled an engagement ring from his pocket, and I realized he was not joking at all!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Later, Brian showed me the most adorable video of himself asking all our animals about proposing to me. I was surprised all over again! And then he finally answered my question about where the real ring came from. I was surprised yet again, and knowing my whole family had been in on this plan for well over a month was fun, too!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"placeholder":"Content…"} -->
<p>Brian and I met during the pandemic. He walked into my chaotic life like it was no big deal and embraced an entirely new lifestyle because of me and my animals. The way he incorporated all of them in this proposal made it so much better and highlighted how easily he opened his heart to all of them from the start. Especially Tucker, my first true love, the one who has stood with me through everything over the last 17 years. As Brian said, Tucker and I have been together for so long, and it will be a long while before Brian can catch up!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></div>

<p>You know, those moments when events seem completely unrelated, yet they somehow connect in surprising ways? Like running into an old friend at a train station, both of you heading in opposite directions. You exchange a few words, and then—out of nowhere—you realize you share a significant friend in common. It’s the six degrees of separation in action.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It all started with Greg, who seems to have a special kind of luck. He wins things, good things happen to him effortlessly—it’s almost like a superpower. This time, he returned from the UK with a beautiful Breyer horse statue he won at a gala dinner for all the ‘Big Wigs’ in the equestrian industry. Greg’s always getting invited to these fancy events.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This Breyer statue was created in honour of the late HRH Queen Elizabeth II. It’s a reissue of the Carltonlima Emma Fell Pony model, featuring a purple and silver ribbon sash with the Queen’s name. Queen Elizabeth was a lifelong equestrian and a patron of the <a href="https://www.fellponysociety.org.uk/about_breed.htm"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Fell Pony Society</span></strong></a>. She often enjoyed riding Emma around the grounds of Windsor Castle, and the beloved pony even attended the Queen’s funeral procession, draped in one of Her Majesty’s headscarves. We were all thrilled to receive this special tribute to The Queen.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->

It’s a stunning shot of the Queen with her beloved pony—but wait, take a closer look! What’s Emma wearing on her hooves?</p>

<p><strong>CAVALLO HOOF BOOTS.</strong></p>

Cavallo has always been at the forefront of the hoof boot industry, leading the way with a deep purpose to help horses. Since 2001, we’ve been changing the world for horses. <h2 class="wp-block-heading">Time for a Poem: 'A Cavallo Christmas'</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>‘Twas the night before Christmas, and under the sky,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In stables all peaceful, the horses did lie.<br>They snuggled in straw, with dreams full of cheer,<br>Of Cavallo Hoof Boots they’d soon hold dear.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The mare, with her coat of a silvery grey,<br>Imagined the boots in the most charming way.<br>How snug they would fit, she thought with a grin,<br>To dance through the snow, with no ice ‘neath my skin!”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The stallion, all regal, with mane flowing free,<br>Dreamed of those boots, sturdy as can be.<br>Through fields I shall gallop, with comfort and grace,<br>No rocks to cause bruises, no mud to disgrace.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The colts, full of wonder, with eyes big and bright,<br>Pictured the boots in the soft moonlight.<br>They’ll keep us so comfy, said one with delight,<br>Bling will make our hooves twinkle in the cold winter night!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As the clock struck the hour, the snowflakes did fall,<br>And magic was stirring in the barn’s wooden hall.<br>By the manger, a whisper — a jingle, a cheer —<br>It was Christmas, and now their Cavallos were here!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Each horse stood in awe as they opened their eyes,<br>To find Cavallo Boots, their delightful surprise!<br>They gleamed and they glittered and fit hooves just right,<br>Their dreams had come true on this cold winter night.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Out of every stable, from dawn’s early light,<br>The horses trotted proudly, their hearts full of might.<br>With Cavallos and studs, they’d race through the snow,<br>A Christmas to remember, with comfort aglow.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So here’s to the horses, both gentle and strong,<br>May their Christmas be merry, and their journeys be long.<br>With Hoof Boots to carry them through winter’s embrace,<br>They’ll gallop and prance with a smile on their face!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>-Your Cavallo Team</p>
