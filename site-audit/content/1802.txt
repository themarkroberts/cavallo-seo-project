<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Bettering the Lives of Miniature Horses</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Over the past five years, the CLB has climbed from being a boot offered in a single size (M2) to be the World’s most recognized and popular miniature hoof boot.&nbsp; Keeping up with demand after the initial CLB launch was challenging.&nbsp; We had underestimated the need for mini boots to cater to our smaller equine friends. We progressed to offer the M3 size in 2016, and escalating demand has continued since. Now there are four sizes on a Slim Sole, all offered in Black or Bling. &nbsp;CLB is not only stylish, but invaluable for its role for a variety of activities. Safety, protection and comfort are synonymous with the household hoof boot brand - Cavallo. Use your CLBs for <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equine-assisted_therapy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">miniature horse therapy</a>, <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QETFqiEpa4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">hoof rehabilitation</a>, <a href="http://cavallo.local/cavallo-clb-offers-minis-safety-and-comfort-while-driving/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">driving on asphalt, gravel</a> and much more!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Keeping Minis Fit and Healthy</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We often get a lot of response from our customers by email and this year we're noticing a trend.&nbsp; Many mini owners are thanking us for developing a boot that allows them to drive twice as far on outings with their minis, keeping their horses at an optimal level of fitness.&nbsp; With the boots on, minis go a longer distance. Without these boots, they would have displayed signs of fatigue or soreness much sooner.&nbsp; The thick soles of the boots simply do the work to absorb the concussive force of a hoof against pavement.&nbsp; Owners and horses are feeling more confident. Improving traction and providing protection goes a long way to improve performance and decrease risks.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Traction on Winter Ground</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Specially formulated TPU soles provide better grip on slick surfaces, such as interior floors and rainy roads.&nbsp; In the winter months, asphalt and other outdoor surfaces can become slick with frost, ice or even snow.&nbsp; To give safety and strong grip on these surfaces, studs can be added to all CLB Boots, and are easily removed once they are no longer required.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Getting Arena-Ready</strong></h3>

He moves great in them and it does not affect his action. He has never had a boot slide off while working. </em></p>
