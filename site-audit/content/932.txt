<p>I currently ride two horses with my Cavallo Saddle Pad and I love the feel it has on them. I can tell what my horse is doing a lot better than when I rode with a regular saddle pad. After I received my pad I rode my horse in it for a month to get use to it, then I rode him in the Coombs Bull-O-Rama and I ended up being the 2004 Champion. I now ride my new horse in it, and he performs better with it, as he gets very hot and sweaty as he works, and the pad keeps him cool.</p>
