<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">Here Comes the Pony!</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image -->
<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/National-Pony-Express-map.jpg" alt="National Pony Express map" title="National Pony Express map"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a href="https://nationalponyexpress.org/about/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">The National Pony Express</a>&nbsp;has fascinated Americans since its first riders hit the leather in April 1860! This mail service lasted only 19 months, but won a permanent place in the history of the American West.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The National Pony Express Association (NPEA) was established in 1978 to honor the memory and endeavors of the Pony Express riders and to identify, preserve, and mark the original Pony Express route.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">National Pony Express Rider Patsy Lange's Report on the 2024 Re-Ride:</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“I have been using Cavallo hoof boots on my daughter’s and my own horses for at least 20 years! I’ve used them on mountain rides of all types of terrain: rocky, muddy, etc. I have never had any of them come off or tear. The main use of the boots for us has been on the National Pony Express Re-ride, which I have participated in for 32 years. This year my Trail was from the Wyoming/Utah border to Murray Park in Salt Lake City, Utah. We try to stay as close to the original Pony Express Trail as possible. I had 13 Riders and we covered about 72 miles in 9 hours.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“The Pony left St. Joseph, Missouri, on 6/17/2024 and arrived in Old Sacramento, California, on 6/27/2024, 10 days later. We ride 24/7, covering 1,966 miles, as the original riders did in 1860. The Pony travels through eight states: Missouri, Kansas, Nebraska, Colorado, Wyoming, Utah, Nevada and California.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“There are approximately 650 to 700 Riders and their horses carrying between 1100 and 1200 historical vignettes and personal letters (YES, you can send a personal letter by Pony Express!). The letters are carried in a mochila, a leather blanket-type device designed to fit securely over a McClellan saddle. It can be easily taken off the saddle and placed on the saddle of the new horse and rider at the relay stations.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Each state has its own Facebook page. There are some great pictures, videos, and stories from this year’s Re-ride and previous Re-rides as well. Our mission is to preserve, mark and maintain the original Trail and to KEEP THE HISTORY ALIVE! See ya up the Trail!”&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>– Patsy Lange, Utah State President</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For more information, please visit the National Pony Express website&nbsp;<strong><a href="http://nationalponyexpress.org/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">HERE!&nbsp;</a></strong></p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
