<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Packed Presentations and Scary Stories (with Good Endings)!</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-bottom:27px">It’s been exciting to be back out with our amazing community again! My three presentations at Equine Affaire in Ohio were super well-attended. There were about 140 people at each one. This was the first time I took questions during my talk, rather than at the end, so we had real conversations about the issues people wanted to know about. Topics like, what happens when we change the feed program, say from a grassy pasture to strictly hay? How long does it take to transition to barefoot and booted? Back at the booth, Greg and I were visited by Cavallo Crusaders all day long! We got to hear so many stories. One story that really stands out was from a woman who thought they were about to ride through a big patch of leaves that actually turned out to be quicksand! Surprise! And when they finally got her horse out, he still had his Cavallos on! So many good friends and wonderful horse people. It was a fabulous four days. I miss everyone already and look forward to going back next year! <strong>A Moment in Time: To get a feel for the electric atmosphere during one of Carole’s Talks at Equine Affaire, WATCH HERE to drop in for a moment!</strong></p>

<p class="has-large-font-size" style="margin-top:0;margin-bottom:0">Carole Herder</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"padding":{"right":"0","left":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="padding-right:0;padding-left:0">President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
