<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="When-to-use-pads">Which Cavallo Hoof Boot Style is Best for Your Horse?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>The right Cavallo Hoof Boot style depends on your horse’s needs, but every model provides key benefits. Cavallo Hoof Boots offer protection and comfort for both barefoot and shod horses. With a replaceable Velcro closure system, they are easy to put on, stay secure, and come off effortlessly.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Each boot features padded leather on the inside heel bulb for comfort and protection against rubbing. The shock-absorbing sole reduces strain on joints, making them ideal for horses with chronic hoof pain or sensitivity. Cavallo boots support rehabilitation from injuries, abscesses, laminitis, and sole bruising. They also serve as a “spare tire” for lost shoes and offer protection during transport, turnout, and breeding.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":4,"className":""} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Slim vs. Regular Soles</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>Cavallo boots come in two sole shapes: Regular and Slim. The Regular Sole is shaped for round hooves, while the Slim Sole is ¼ inch (6mm) narrower, designed for more oval-shaped hooves. Both feature a built-in breakover and a tread pattern for traction on all terrains. Choosing the correct sole shape ensures the best fit, maximizing comfort and performance.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>With durable materials and flexible design, Cavallo boots allow natural hoof movement while promoting circulation and hoof health. No matter the style, they provide reliable protection and support for your horse’s every step.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo TREK Hoof Boots</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Cavallo Trek is the most popular and versatile hoof boot on the market! Designed for all riding disciplines and terrain types, it features a breathable Pro Mesh upper made from tough, flexible TPU material that resists dirt and debris while allowing airflow to keep hooves cool. The Trek’s replaceable Velcro closure system ensures a secure fit and makes adjustments easy.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>The Trek is ideal for all disciplines, offering durability and flexibility on rocky, steep, or varied terrain. Its design works great on all horses, with front flaps that can be angled either upwards or downwards to fit both heavy and fine-boned horses. Furthermore, it supports horses recovering from hoof conditions such as abscesses, bruises, or laminitis by providing a cushioned, protective barrier. The Trek boot is Cavallo's top choice for rehabilitation.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Barefoot horses benefit from the Trek’s protective design, which shields hooves while allowing natural function. In addition, it can also be used over metal shoes to add shock absorption and prevent excessive wear. Suitable for intense arena work, trail riding, turnout, and trailering, the Trek boot is also an excellent choice for jumping and high-speed events like barrel racing. Riders looking for comfort, protection, and reliability in any situation will find the Trek boot an outstanding option.</p>
