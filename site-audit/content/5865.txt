<h2 class="wp-block-heading">When to use Cavallo Pastern Wraps?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":6} -->
<h6 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo Pastern Wraps are ideal when your horse needs extra protection, comfort, or cushioning around the pastern and heel bulbs. Here are the key times and situations to use them!</h6>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Riding and High-Intensity Work</strong> for horses engaged in high-intensity activities, Pastern Wraps add protection against rubbing. The thin neoprene reduces friction and shields sensitive areas (such as your horse's heel bulbs and pastern) during repeated movements or exposure to rough terrain.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Full-Time Boot Wear</strong><br>Horses that need to wear Hoof Boots full-time should always use Pastern Protection. These are horses managing chronic conditions like laminitis, founder, navicular, or thin soles. Pastern protection reduces friction on sensitive areas, ensuring comfort and minimizing the risk of irritation during constant boot wear. Pastern Wraps offers a great, easy-to-use solution. They can also be usedto minimize dirt or debris from getting inside the boots.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Sensitive or Thin-Skinned Horses</strong><br>Horses with sensitive or thin skin are more prone to discomfort. This is especially true when first transitioning to Hoof Boots, or when wearing boots for prolonged amounts of time. Cavallo Pastern Wraps offer a soft, cushioned barrier between the Hoof Boot and your horse’s skin, which can prevent irritation. While Cavallo Hoof Boots already provide excellent protection on their own with a gentle, padded leather back designed to shield your horse’s hooves, Pastern Wraps add that additional layer of extra comfort.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Break-In Period for New Boots</strong><br>Even when well-fitted, horses may go through an adjustment period while getting used to wearing Hoof Boots. During this break-in period, Pastern Wraps add extra protection and comfort, allowing your horse to gradually adapt to the new boots. This helps prevent initial discomfort or potential chafing while your horse’s skin and hooves adjust to the secure fit.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">How to Properly Size and Fit Cavallo Pastern Wraps</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">To ensure Cavallo Pastern Wraps fit securely and comfortably, start by <a href="http://cavallo.local/product/pastern-wraps/">choosing a size that matches your hoof boot size</a>. For example, size Small fits boot sizes 0-1, Medium fits boot sizes 2-3 and Large fits sizes 4-6. For heavier-boned or feathered horses, we recommend moving up one size. If your horse is in a size 6 boot but the Large Pastern Wraps are too tight, move up to the BFB Pastern Wraps.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Position approximately 1/4 inch of the wrap under the heel’s weight-bearing points, which secures the wrap during movement. Wraps should fit snugly around the pastern with the Velcro fastened, allowing natural movement while providing full coverage without restriction.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Check Cavallo Pastern Wraps for comfort after fitting. Shifting, bunching, or gaps may mean the size needs adjusting. Checking regularly ensures a proper fit and maintains comfort, especially for horses wearing boots full-time. Pastern Wraps fit comfortably inside Cavallo Boots without significantly altering the boot’s fit. If your horse is between boot sizes, test the fit with and without wraps to determine the best size.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo Pastern Wraps work with all Cavallos except CLB "Cute Little Boots". If you need pastern protection for your mini's CLBs, we recommend purchasing a thick, wool, child-sized sock.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
