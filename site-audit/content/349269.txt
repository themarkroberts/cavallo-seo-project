<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center has-primary-color has-text-color has-link-color">Cavallo Case Study: </h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"style":{"typography":{"textAlign":"center"}}} -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">Returning a Senior Pony to Comfort and Work After Laminitis</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If you’ve ever watched a beloved horse or pony struggle to walk comfortably and felt the heartbreak of seeing them stiff, sore, or reluctant to move… Or if you’ve worried about what the future holds for an aging horse dealing with Laminitis - this story will speak directly to you.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Laminitis can feel overwhelming. The pain, the setbacks, the constant management, and the fear that your horse may never fully return to comfort or activity again can leave you feeling helpless. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In this inspiring case study, you’ll meet Knight — a 22-year-old pony with a long history of Laminitis who faced one of his toughest episodes yet. Discover how a thoughtful recovery plan, dedicated owners, and the right hoof support helped him regain comfort, mobility, and even return to doing the work he loves!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

Because when horses can move comfortably, healing can truly begin!</p>
