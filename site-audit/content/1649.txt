It’s THE ultimate test of horse and rider! One event, three disciplines, testing the resolve of the timeless partnership between horse and rider – <a href="http://rk3de.org/visitors-guide/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ROLEX</a> is this weekend!

It’s at the very beautiful Kentucky Horse Park and we, Cavallo will be there!

This is our first time at such an event and we are excited.

It’s these types of events that help keep horses employed by maintaining and generating an interest in horses that should not be overshadowed by technology and life’s fast pace.

<a href="http://rk3de.org/equestrian-events-inc-increases-donations-local-equine-charities/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Equestrain Events Inc.(EEI)</a> mission is to support the development of riders and horses in one or more of the sports recognized by the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Federation_for_Equestrian_Sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Fédération Equestre Internationale (FEI)</a>.

DRESSAGE is on Thursday and Friday - beauty, grace, elegance.

CROSS-COUNTRY, the highlight, is on Saturday – exciting, hazardous, challenging.

JUMPING TEST, the final phase, is on Sunday – energy, precision, athleticism.

Please come visit with us at Rolex, where we are partnered with our good friends at The <a href="https://www.valleyvet.com/ct_search_results.html?gas=cavallo" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Valley Vet Supply</a> Booth #144.
