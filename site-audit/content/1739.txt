<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Monty Roberts - Healing PTSD</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>One of our biggest passions is Equine Assisted Therapy (EAT).&nbsp; Our good friend Monty touches our hearts with this regular program that he runs - 'Horse Sense and Healing'.&nbsp; This workshop is geared specifically to veterans, police, fire fighters, first responders or their family who are dealing with the effects of having experienced trauma or have symptoms of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD).&nbsp; Monty does not charge participants for this workshop, and you can bet that the attendees experience amazing results!</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Meanwhile, Back at the Ranch...</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And what's happening here at Cavallo?&nbsp; Jenny got a new dog a few months back, which she adopted from <a href="https://www.facebook.com/blackdogrescuegroup/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Black Dog Rescue Society of BC</a>.&nbsp; This rescue brings dogs from the Houston Texas area, where so many dogs are euthanized due to over-population.&nbsp; Jenny describes the adoption process as "basically exactly like online dating but with a bigger commitment and better results", as the dogs are adopted sight-unseen after they are posted online.&nbsp; It was love at first sight when Jenny went to pick up little Panda!&nbsp; She's an energetic dog who is thriving in our rain forest setting here in British Columbia.&nbsp; And our dog Danny-Boy is also a winner in this situation as he has gained a new friend!</p>
