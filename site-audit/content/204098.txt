<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Beyoncé: Queen of Hoof Boots</h2>

<p style="margin-top:16px;margin-bottom:0px">Did you know Beyoncé rocked more than just the stage at the iconic NFL "Beyoncé Bowl"? In a dazzling twist that had the equestrian world buzzing, Cavallo Trek Boots made a surprise appearance—on her horse! Yes, you read that right. The same boots trusted by trail riders and performance horses alike were a prominent part of Beyoncé's entrance.
