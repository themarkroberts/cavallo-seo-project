<h2>Emma Massingale</h2>
Meet Emma Massingale –  a true horse lover and adventuress, free-rider Emma is continually pushing the boundaries of the equine and human relationship.   Last year, Emma lived with horses, wild and tame, for one month on an island.   See <a href="http://www.theislandproject.emmamassingale.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">www.theislandproject.emmamassingale.com</a> for more information.
Emma and her horses love Cavallo Trek Boots.  <img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-6668" src="//cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Libert-Ponies-3-300x200.jpg" alt="Libert Ponies 3" width="300" height="200" />
As Emma says, <em>“Their boots are making such a difference to their training , now we can literally go anywhere! I didn’t really realise how restricting life was before!”
</em>

This year, Emma is travelling through Scotland with a team of ponies without bridles, saddles or halters;  but all the ponies will have on Cavallos!  As she says:  “<em>My personal ethos &lt;is&gt;  No Reins…No Rules…No limits!! Simply for the love of horses!”</em>

Follow Emma’s adventures on her website <a href="http://www.emmamassingale.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">http://www.emmamassingale.com/</a> .

We can’t wait to see what Emma chooses as her next project!
