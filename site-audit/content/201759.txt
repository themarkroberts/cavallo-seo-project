<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What Are The Benefits of Using Cavallo Hoof Boots For Shod Horses?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Increased Traction</strong> - On rugged trails, hard ground, and pavement, metal shoes often slip, which increases the risk of discomfort or injury. Cavallo Hoof Boots provide a tough, durable sole with excellent traction, reducing the likelihood of slipping. This added grip ensures better stability and improves your horse’s confidence on challenging terrain. Whether navigating wet rocks, mud, or smooth pavement, Cavallo Hoof Boots help your horse move securely and comfortably.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Scientifically Proven Shock Absorption and Pressure Reduction</strong> - Cavallo Hoof Boots are scientifically proven to reduce pressure and absorb shock, making them especially beneficial for shod horses. An<strong> <a href="http://cavallo.local/dr-jennifer-gill-western-kentucky-university-hoof-boot-study/">independent study by Weste</a></strong><a href="http://cavallo.local/dr-jennifer-gill-western-kentucky-university-hoof-boot-study/"><strong>rn Kentucky University </strong></a>(WKU) used FujiFilm imaging to confirm that Cavallo Trek Hoof Boots significantly decrease hoof pressure and increase surface contact with the ground compared to traditional steel horseshoes. Since metal shoes concentrate pressure and limit natural shock absorption, Cavallo Hoof Boots act as a protective barrier—reducing impact, improving circulation, and enhancing comfort for shod horses. With a thick tread and advanced design, these boots help dissipate force on hard terrain, making them the ideal choice for protecting shod hooves and promoting overall hoof health.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Extra Sole Protection and Coverage</strong> - Cavallo Hoof Boots offer extra protection for shod horses, especially those with thin or sensitive soles. The full-coverage design shields the hoof and heel, reducing wear and guarding against sharp or uneven terrain. By preventing direct contact with hard ground or rocks, the boots help minimize bruising and hoof damage. Various padding options for inside the boots make them an excellent option for rehabilitative care or extended use.</p>
