<!-- /wp:heading -->

<p>There’s something about donkeys that makes people soften.<br><br>Maybe it’s their expression, that calm, watchful gaze that feels timeless and ancient.<br><br>Maybe it’s their long ears, swiveling like little radar dishes, catching every sound. Or maybe it’s the quiet strength and knowingness they carry.</p>
