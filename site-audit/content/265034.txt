<h3 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">Wrap them in Love: Why Cavallo Pastern Wraps Treat your Horse</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:columns {"style":{"spacing":{"padding":{"top":"0","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<div class="wp-block-columns" style="padding-top:0;padding-bottom:0"><!-- wp:column {"verticalAlignment":"center","width":"50%"} -->
<div class="wp-block-column is-vertically-aligned-center" style="flex-basis:50%"><!-- wp:image {"id":265048,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/Pastern-Wraps-on-Hoofs-2.jpg" alt="Cavallo Hoof Boots - Pastern Wraps on Hoofs " class="wp-image-265048"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image --></div>
<!-- /wp:column -->

<!-- wp:column {"verticalAlignment":"top","width":"","style":{"spacing":{"padding":{"right":"0","left":"0","top":"0","bottom":"0"}}},"layout":{"type":"constrained","contentSize":"585px","justifyContent":"right","wideSize":""}} -->
<div class="wp-block-column is-vertically-aligned-top" style="padding-top:0;padding-right:0;padding-bottom:0;padding-left:0"><!-- wp:group {"layout":{"type":"constrained"}} -->
<div class="wp-block-group"><!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"normal"} -->
<p class="has-normal-font-size">Cavallo Pastern Wraps are designed to provide a soft, protective barrier between your horse’s skin and the hoof boot collar. While Cavallos are engineered for comfort and durability, certain conditions may cause friction:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Horses with sensitive skin</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Longer rides on rugged terrain</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Horses new to wearing hoof boots</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Wet or sandy conditions where grit can work its way in</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>By wrapping the pastern, you reduce the chance of rubbing, chafing, or hair loss — so your horse stays happy, protected, and ready to ride.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list --></div>
<!-- /wp:group --></div>
<!-- /wp:column --></div>
<!-- /wp:columns -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"left","level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-left">How Pastern Wraps Prevent Rubbing &amp; Keep Your Horse Comfortable</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Think of pastern wraps as your horse’s “safety net.” They don’t just prevent rubbing — they boost the overall performance of your hoof boots. Here’s how:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cushioning: Extra padding where it matters most.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Protection: Shields against grit, sand, or small stones.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Security: Helps the boots fit more snugly without adding bulk.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Versatility: Easy to slip on and off, washable, and reusable.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Many riders find that pastern wraps extend the life of their boots by preventing unnecessary wear and tear around the collar.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"left","level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-left">Wondering How to Use Pastern Wraps With Your Cavallos?</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:columns -->
<div class="wp-block-columns"><!-- wp:column {"width":"66.66%"} -->
<div class="wp-block-column" style="flex-basis:66.66%"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Not every horse needs pastern wraps all the time — but here are a few situations where they really shine:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>During the break-in period: When your horse is new to Cavallo Hoof Boots, wraps provide extra comfort until the boots “settle in.”</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>On long trail rides: Particularly on rocky or uneven terrain, where movement and grit can increase friction.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>With sensitive horses: If your horse is prone to rubs or has thinner hair around the pastern.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>In wet or muddy conditions: Pastern Wraps help keep debris out and prevent moisture-related rubbing.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In other words, pastern wraps are like a trusty trail companion — you may not always need them, but when you do, you’ll be glad they’re there.</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
