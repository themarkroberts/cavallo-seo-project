<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Why Accurate Measuring for Cavallo Hoof Boots Matters</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Above all else, sizing and measuring properly for Cavallo Hoof Boots is the key to success. A perfect fit keeps the boot snug and secure without rubbing or twisting. Cavallo boots are designed to follow the natural shape of the hoof, so getting these measurements right ensures that your horse gets the comfort and support they deserve.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Most Important: Always measure after a fresh trim.</strong> The Cavallo size chart already accounts for normal growth between trims, so don’t guess or pad the numbers. Firstly, clean your horse's hoof, removing any dirt, rocks, or debris. Secondly, identify the buttress lines (weight-bearing points of the heel) and measure from the toe to the buttress line precisely, using a ruler marked in millimetres or 1/16-inch increments. Precision makes all the difference!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Furthermore, Cavallo offers two sole shapes: Regular, for rounder hooves (equal length and width), and Slim, for narrower feet (1/4" or 6mm longer than wide). As a result, choosing the correct sole shape prevents twisting and ensures proper weight-bearing support. Remember, even on the same horse, each hoof can be a little different, so measure each hoof individually.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></div>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Step-by-Step How to Measure Your Horse’s Hooves for Cavallo Hoof Boots</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">Sizing and measuring with Cavallo Hoof Boots is easy! Therefore, all you will need is a ruler, a clean, freshly trimmed hoof, and a camera if you would like free, expert sizing advice. Firstly, start with length, and place the zero end of the ruler at the very tip of the toe. Measure straight up the center of your horse's hoof to the buttress line. But do not include the heel bulbs, as this will make your measurement too long. This is your horse's length.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></div>
<!-- /wp:column --></div>
<!-- /wp:columns -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Secondly, measure the width across the widest point of the hoof. Keep the ruler level and snug against both sides of the object. Don’t angle it, and don’t guess! Cavallo boots are sized by length, but width helps you choose between Regular and Slim soles. If your horse’s width is ¼ inch (6mm) less than the length, go with a Slim sole.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"32px","bottom":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:0">Need help? No problem! Cavallo’s support team is happy to review your horse's hoof photos and recommend the right size. Take a clear photo of each measurement. Moreover, Good lighting, straight angles, and clean hooves are key. Even better - ask a friend to help hold the hoof. </p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Special Cases When Sizing and Measuring for Cavallo Hoof Boots: Shod Hooves, Fine-Boned Horses &amp; More</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Not only for barefoot horses, Cavallo Hoof Boots can be used to provide additional comfort and protection over metal horseshoes for shod horses, too. To measure over horseshoes, measure the shoe itself, not the bare hoof. For example, place the ruler on the outer edges of the horseshoe and follow the same steps for length and width. Consequently, Cavallo Hoof Boots protect your horse's shod hooves from concussion, heel bruising, and slipping too - and they're great for transport, turnout, or rehab!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Furthermore, for fine-boned horses, the Cavallo Trek and ELB styles offer an extra-snug fit. Their wrap-around closures allow you to create a V-shape that tightens the boot collar, providing a secure fit around the pastern. Similarly, for horses with a heavier boned, Cavallo Simple and Sport boots are a fantastic option, featuring a roomier upper.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In conclusion, no matter your horse’s shape, size, or shoeing status, Cavallo has a boot that fits! And if you’re ever unsure, please don't hesitate to reach out. Above all, the Cavallo team is always here to help guide you to the correct size with confidence!</p>
