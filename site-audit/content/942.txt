<strong>New Cavallo Sports Chair with Side Table
</strong>

The new Cavallo lightweight aluminum frame Sports Chair with attached side table and convenient adjustable shoulder strap is portable and easy to take along on your trips.

The whole chair, including the convenient compact side table, folds in for packing up and storage.

When you get to your outdoor destination, just unfold your comfy chair, open your favourite drink, use your side table for a game of cards or your laptop and enjoy the convenience. It even has a side organizer with several storage pockets for sunglasses, i-pod, etc.

This is a must have for the trail rider and camper or even just around the home.
