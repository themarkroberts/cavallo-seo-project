<p style="text-align: justify;">"I stopped in your booth yesterday and discussed the changes you've made in the Velcro on the newer boots. Last summer I was riding with a friend on an asphalt trail. I had front Cavallo boots on so my horse didn't chip his hooves. It was hot out and we decided to take a side path down to the river to get them a drink. At the bank of the river, my gelding suddenly sunk up to his belly in quicksand and rotted leaves. I quickly stepped off. After a few minutes of struggling, he was able to pull himself out. I thought sure his boots would be gone. But no, there they were, still firmly attached. I couldn't believe it! Thank you for making a great product!"   - Diane Colvin</p>
