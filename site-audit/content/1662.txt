<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #d61c1c; text-decoration: underline;">Cavallo is NOW The Official Hoof Boot of the RMHA! </span></span></h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="color: #008080;">Celebrating the Rocky Mountain Horse Breed</span></h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We, at Cavallo, are thrilled to announce our partnership with&nbsp;the Rocky Mountain Horse Association! &nbsp;I was recently re-introduced to the Rocky Mountain horse at Equine Affair in Ohio. This Breed is exceptionally easy on the eyes with their rich chocolate coloured body and delightfully golden mane and tail. They boast an exquisite and calm personality. &nbsp; A little lower to the ground, with an ambling 4- beat gait; they are the ideal size for adults wanting to comfortably while-away the hours on the trails of our beautiful country or children just starting out. &nbsp; Not that they can’t perform. They can! &nbsp;It’s just that they don’t, when YOU don’t want them to.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As you know, Cavallo exists to make horses more comfortable, happy and protected. &nbsp;We joined with Rocky Mountain, to highlight the awareness of this amazing breed.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="color: #008080;">It’s Trail Riding Season!</span></h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Through our sponsorship, more ‘Rockys’ can hit the trail comfortably sporting Cavallo Hoof Boots to protect their feet over and through any terrain at any speed and to care for the other “hot spot” for horses, Cavallo ensures comfort under saddle with Cavallo Total Comfort Saddle Pads. It’s our way of giving back and celebrating the Rocky Mountain Horse Breed.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
