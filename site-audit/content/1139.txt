June 1-3 was Amerequine at the Will Rogers Memorial Center in Fort Worth, Texas. President Carole Herder was a presenter on all 3 days of the event, discussing “The Benefits of Barefoot.”  A highlight of the show was meeting and working with “Taffy,” a beautiful rescue mare.  DVM Jennifer Williams from Bluebonnet Equine Humane Society was kind enough to donate her time and this beautiful rescue horse to show the audience how to measure, size and fit for Cavallo Hoof Boots.

Taffy had never had boots on before and at first lifted her foot high, but after a few turns in the ring she relaxed and enjoyed the comfort that Cavallo Hoof Boots provide! The wonderful staff at Bluebonnet Equine Humane Society share in Cavallo’s mission to improve the lives of equines everywhere!
