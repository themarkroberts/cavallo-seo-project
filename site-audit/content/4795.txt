<h3 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center" style="padding-bottom:var(--wp--preset--spacing--md)">HEAR ME SPEAK: I'm a Presenter at Equine Affaire Ohio this Week!</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It’s an all-star list of presenters this year, including <a href="https://guymclean.store/pages/about-us" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Guy McLean</span></a>, <a href="https://signin.juliegoodnight.com/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Julie Goodnight</span></a> and <a href="https://jonathanfieldhorsemanship.com/about/#tab-id-3" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Jonathan Field</span></a>!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Where &amp; When?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>My presentation <strong>Not a Vet? Here’s an Easy Insight to Laminitis</strong> takes place Friday at 12pm at the Seminar Stage (Voinovich Center), and my second talk, <strong>8 Most Important Horse Care Concerns and How to Handle Them</strong> will be on Friday at 5pm in the Demo Ring (Celeste Center). <a href="https://equineaffaire.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/23-OH-Schedule-032323.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Check out the presenter’s schedule HERE!</span></a></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>What Will You Learn?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>My first talk is tailored to the non-professional horse owner for the prevention, understanding and treatment of Laminitis. Listeners will receive a simple definition of the disease, a list and variety of its causes and a good arsenal of tools to employ should the illness strike. Although Laminitis can inflict merciless agony and is potentially fatal, it is curable with an understanding of realistic and appropriate information. Knowledge provides power and independence.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I’ll give an overview of horse care topics, including supplements, digestive and muscular systems, shelter and suitable environment options, training and innate behaviours, hoof function, lameness prevention and common sense.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Certain simple laws of nature and science support your horse’s hoofs and overall health. Understanding hoof function will help you ward off problems such as Laminitis, Navicular Disease, skeletal and muscular fitness and gut health. Even teeth, performance and attitude are affected by the health of the hoof.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo FAQ Time!</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Question: What's a Wonder-ful Way to Fit Test your Cavallos?</strong><br>We always ask our customers to protect their new boots to keep them in “as new” condition when trying them on for the first time. This is important in case you need to exchange your boots for another size.&nbsp;</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Trying them on a soft surface (dry grass, on a rug, etc.) is ideal, so the soles cannot be scratched. It’s also important to cover the hoof with thin plastic to keep them nice and clean.&nbsp;</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
