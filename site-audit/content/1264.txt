<p>As a major supporter of the American Competitive Trail Horse Association (ACTHA), since the beginning over 5 years ago, we just love the contribution ACTHA makes to various horse rescues all over the USA. Now in addition Cavallo has partnered with the AIM group in their efforts to provide a Home For Every Horse. We feel very fortunate to be able to provide this level of support and it’s because of YOU – you who purchase Cavallo products. You make it possible.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So now that’s about horses, but what about dogs? We have just become aware of a special appeal for rescuing dogs in the Dominican Republic. It’s pretty sad what’s going on over there, but we are assured that 100% of funds donated are going directly to this effort. It was sort of a “no brainer” when we saw this video.<br>
This is not about Cavallo asking you to do anything. It’s just keeping you in touch about our efforts to help.</p>
