<h3 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center" style="margin-top:64px;font-style:normal;font-weight:600">The Science Behind Cavallo Hoof Boots</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:group {"style":{"spacing":{"padding":{"top":"0","bottom":"0","left":"0","right":"0"},"margin":{"top":"24px","bottom":"0"}}},"layout":{"type":"constrained","contentSize":"937px"}} -->
<div class="wp-block-group" style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:0;padding-top:0;padding-right:0;padding-bottom:0;padding-left:0"><!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p class="has-text-align-center">An independent study by Western Kentucky University (WKU) confirmed that Cavallo Trek Hoof Boots offer exceptional hoof protection, traction, and pressure reduction. Using FujiFilm imaging, researchers found that these boots significantly decrease hoof pressure and increase surface contact with the ground, compared to traditional steel horseshoes. The thicker tread and enhanced protection of Cavallo Boots make them ideal for hard terrain, providing better stability, comfort, and force dissipation for horses. This research validates Cavallo's reputation for superior hoof care.</p>
