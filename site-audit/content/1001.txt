After an exciting day at The Functional Hoof Conference in Australia last week, we relax with a cold beer, a meat pie and a game of Footie – how Australian! We are not chatting about the fabulous research on the wild Brumbies of the outback. We are not speaking about the horse’s hoofs, hoof boots, saddle pads or horse health or the amazing technical information on laminitis developed by Professor Dr Chris Pollitt.  Instead PhD Brian Hampson casually assures me there will be no man eating crocodiles when anyone ventures out on the Brumby tour next year. Wouldn't that be fun? Chris and I indulge in a little gossip about notorious icons of the horse industry who we know in common. Could you be one of them?
