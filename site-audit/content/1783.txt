<!-- wp:heading {"level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><strong><span style="color: #000000;">Travel Well, Travel Safe, Travel Protected.&nbsp; </span></strong></h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Did you know that more than 50% of the injuries horses sustain in transportation are to the lower limbs? Ranging in severity, from treatable surface wounds to irreparable impairment, the main causes are scrambling, loss of balance, and conflict with other horses.</span></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":4} -->
<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="color: #000000;">Why Risk It?</span></h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">The vibration of travel can aggravate your horses’ joints, muscular and skeletal structure, making your horse sore and stiff.
