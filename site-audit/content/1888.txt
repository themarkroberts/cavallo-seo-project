<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="color: #000000;">Older Horses Face Unique Issues</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Ageing equines often suffer from arthritis, as well as age-related metabolic disorders that can affect their bones, joints, and hooves. <a style="color: #000000;" href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/">All styles of Cavallo Boots</a> offer senior equines enhanced comfort as they move. The thick Cavallo soles absorb shock, re-distribute weight and pressure, and help encourage blood circulation—all essential ingredients for health and longevity. While some older horses can be ridden lightly, others are retired earlier than their owners would like. With extra support, these horses may have a chance to move freely again.</span></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">One thing I know for certain is that bodies feel better when they move. And, yes, this goes for horses too. Movement lubricates joints, increases blood circulation and aids in the distribution of oxygen and nutrients throughout the body. The more horses move, the greater the opportunity to extend their working active lives. Older horses are more willing when movement doesn’t cause pain.</span></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Our boots, combined with optional cushioned pads, offer support for your horse's hooves, as well as joints. Cavallo Boots are also easy on ageing riders’ hands! If arthritis or bending down is an issue, these boots fasten simply, quickly and securely. Read on to find out how hoof boots helped three ageing equines move easily and get back on the trails!</span></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Back Out of Retirement</strong></span></h3>

<!-- wp:image {"id":63827,"align":"left"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image alignleft"><img src="http://cavallo.local/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Marie2-225x300-2.jpg" alt="Marie Reimer's Older Senior Horse in Cavallo Hoof Boots" class="wp-image-63827"/></figure>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Riding with Trek boots helps older horses—and<em> any</em> horses—feel cushioned support while riding on any trail terrain.</span></p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Marie Reimer rides with the Back Country Horsemen of British Columbia. Having <a style="color: #000000;" href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/trek-hoof-boots/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Trek Boots</a>&nbsp;helped her rekindle her riding relationship with her 26-year-old quarter horse, Ricky.</span></p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><em>“I ended up taking Ricky on a four-day camping trip,” she says. “My five-year-old horse wouldn’t load, so Ricky was pressed into service.”</em></span></p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Reimer says Ricky stepped out nicely and moved better than he has in recent days while wearing the Trek Boots.</span></p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><em>“I’m going to take him out more, based on this trip,”</em> she says. <em>“We did some steep terrain at one point, and he had no trouble with grip. I am really impressed. Plus, we did trot some, went into the lake for him to drink and I didn’t worry about the boots coming off.”</em></span></p>

<p><span style="color: #000000;"><em>“We rode a small bit of the <a style="color: #000000;" href="https://mc2ct.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Mokelumne Coast to Crest Trail</a> in the lower foothills of <a style="color: #000000;" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calaveras_County,_California" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Calaveras County, California</a>,”</em> Fish says. <em>“Josie is now breaking in her Trek Boots that I will be using from here on out on our adventures.
