<h2 class="wp-block-heading">A Valentine's Day Gift for You &amp; Your Equine</h2>

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-bottom:27px"><strong>We have a gift for you</strong> – a gift we hope you won’t need, but that we know will help if you do. Have you ever battled Laminitis, or is it something you worry could happen?</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Healing from Laminitis can be a tough journey for a horse, and for us horse owners, it can be even more so. We feel their pain. This journey can be confusing, too, as you’ll find that recovery isn’t always a straight trajectory, but a combination of pieces that fall into place.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Where have Greg and I Been Lately?</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size"><strong>Short answer… Saskatchewan!</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Long answer: At a once-in-a-lifetime ceremony!</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Our son recently graduated as a Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP) member, bringing much honour to our family. After completing an intense and gruelling 26-week training program at Depot, the RCMP Academy in Regina, Saskatchewan, he now has the distinction of being a working RCMP officer, serving his community and our country.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><a href="https://www.rcmp-grc.gc.ca/depot/cadet-chronicles-journal-cadet/graduation-promotion-eng.htm" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Graduation Day</span></a> was an exciting and emotional experience we will never forget. A day of ceremony steeped in teamwork and tradition was an outstanding way to commemorate the dedication, long days and hard work these cadets put in at Depot. We couldn’t have been more proud of our son, seeing him march in his <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Serge" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">red serge uniform.</span></a></p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">New Book by Sue Grice: The Training Spiral</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size">"With a happier horse and a happier rider, training should progress more quickly." - Sue Grice</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"normal"} -->
<p class="has-normal-font-size">The “Scales of Training,” “Training Scale,” and “Training Pyramid” are familiar terms to riders as a part of their equestrian education.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Dressage rider and trainer Sue Grice grew up with these concepts, too, and like many, found them to be not only fairly boring, but also confusing when it came to actually implementing them as part of daily training. She came to realize that few actually applied them in practice.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Sue determined that this was because there was really no clear framework or methodology for implementing the system in a progressive manner. She conceived a new approach based on the standard, but with a focus on a better rate of success. Success could be achieved when the rider was engaged with the system and understood how to continually adhere to it, even as advances (or falters) might occur.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For over 30 years she has continued to develop and apply her ideas, and now Sue has finally brought them together to share with the world.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo FAQ Time!</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"normal"} -->
<p class="has-normal-font-size">How will my horse react to having boots on his feet for the first time?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Horses’ ancient survival mechanism depends on their ability to outrun predators. Healthy hoofs are crucial to survival. When you first put Cavallos on, your horse may lift his feet with curiosity and then, almost immediately, become aware that his soles are protected and more comfortable. His head drops, eyes relax, and the tongue comes out licking. He becomes calm and thankful.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Here’s a testimonial sent to us from a first-time boot user:</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“I was blown away how quickly my Mare, Layla, showed relief wearing her Trek Boots…it took 40 seconds only. She licked and chewed at about 15-20 seconds wearing them, then started moving faster.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Her story was: my farrier had to trim aggressively to get rid of some bad splitting. My farrier suggested getting some boots to help for the transition till new growth. She was on Bute but now is pain-free. The boots also have the&nbsp;<a href="http://cavallo.local/product/comfort-pad/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Comfort Pads</a>&nbsp;in them, which I think made a huge difference. She holds her feet up patiently when I check them, she loves them. She’s a healthy mare but those Boots were a miracle. She’s trotting &amp; doing all again totally pain-free…I’ll be ordering a set of boots for my gelding too. Plus now my rides should be even better!” – Elke East of Calgary, Alberta</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
