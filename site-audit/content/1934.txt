<p>No matter where in Florida this mini horse therapy team travels, they wear their <a href="http://cavallo.local/product-category/hoof-boot/mini-hoof-boots/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cavallo CLB Boots</a> during therapy visits!</p>

<p>Thirty-two-inch tall,<a href="https://minitherapy.org/#ourHorses" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> “Honey”</a> is the miniature horse who started it all. Mary Rose Gullet of <a href="https://minitherapy.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Honey's Mini Therapy Adventures</a> says people are always excited to see their mini horses and are mesmerized by their sparkly, 'cute little boots'. All of us here at Cavallo feel very proud to sponsor Honey's, and we always look forward to seeing their latest adventures in the media and online.</p>

<h4 class="wp-block-heading">How Does Honey's Operate as an Organization?</h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Honey’s Mini Therapy Adventures is a 501c3 organization based in central Florida. Their organization was designed around a therapeutic model wherein individuals of all ages, gender, and race with physical, cognitive, emotional, developmental, and behavioural disabilities find comfort, empowerment, self-esteem, and unconditional love through positive interventions with miniature horses. They have connected with multiple facilities across central Florida and have expanded the program rapidly, reaching people in need from all aspects of life.</p>
