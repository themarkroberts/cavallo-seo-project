<p>My heart was broken when they put my horse down. Rocky was badly lame with ringbone, sidebone, splints and arthritis. He also had ulcers from the pain meds he was on. <!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<p>The painful death of my first horse was a pivotal point in my life. I became committed – in fact, obsessed- with finding answers.
