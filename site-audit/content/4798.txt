<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Barefoot Mounted Units Race from Houston to Austin</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>From Amy Blackwelder:</strong> “Thanks for sharing my photo. My partner Anson Drake &amp; I loped 1 mile at a time as a team in a relay-style race. Both of our horses are barefoot and were wearing Cavallo boots the whole race! The Montgomery County Sheriff’s Office Mounted Unit proudly participated in the 75th Harris County Sheriff’s Office Pony Express. The Pony Express Ride is a prestigious tradition where horses and riders, mainly from Houston-area law enforcement mounted patrol agencies, travel along U.S. Highway 290 from Waller County Fairgrounds to the State Capital in a relay fashion. Once they reach the Capitol in Austin, riders pass on letters of invitations to elected officials on the steps of the Capitol, marking the beginning of the 92nd Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo.”</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Rethinking Stall Rest - The Humble Hoof Podcast</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">When your horse comes up lame, one of the first instincts is to limit movement to ensure something catastrophic doesn’t happen or more damage isn’t done. But is this the right approach?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px">Dr. Martina Neidhart is a veterinarian in Europe with an interest in soft tissue and lameness rehabilitation. The Humble Hoof’s Alicia Harlov reached out to Dr. Neidhart to create <a href="https://thehumblehoof.com/2024/02/23/the-pros-and-cons-of-stall-rest/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">this podcast episode</span></a> about when stall rest might be recommended, when we should limit a horse’s movement, and in what instances it might not be necessary. They also discuss what can happen to horses who don’t move enough while rehabbing.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Find out how Cavallos can help keep your horse comfy and moving during a hoof rehab situation <a href="http://cavallo.local/why-trek-makes-the-perfect-therapy-rehab-hoof-boot/"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">HERE</span></a>!</strong></p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Do You Find Joy in the Non-Ridden Horse?</h2>

<p class="has-large-font-size" style="margin-top:0;margin-bottom:0">Carole Herder</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"padding":{"right":"0","left":"0"}}}} -->
<p style="padding-right:0;padding-left:0">President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
