<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Guy McLean – And Excellent Judge of Horse Equipment</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Guy McLean – International horseman, entertainer and poet, is also 2-time World Champion of Road to the Horse! He loves his Cavallo Horse Hoof Boots on rugged terrain and slippery surfaces. Guy is an expert horseman and an excellent judge of horses and horse equipment. His horses do not wear metal horseshoes. Guy likes the reliability of his Cavallos and respects Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc. as a good, honest company.</p>
