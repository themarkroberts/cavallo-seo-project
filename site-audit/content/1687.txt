<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Horse Radio Network Radiothon - Not to be missed!</strong></h4>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cavallo is a proud sponsor of the <a href="http://radiothon.horseradionetwork.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">3rd Annual 12 Hour Live Horse Radio Network Holiday Radiothon</a>.&nbsp; Over $4,000 in equine related prizes will be offered during this exciting event!&nbsp; There will also be twenty-four featured equestrian guests and hundreds of listener calls, songs and poems. Be sure to mark your calendar - this festive online radio event will take place on Nov. 27<sup>th</sup>, at 9:00am EST. <a href="http://www.horsesinthemorning.com/hosts/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Glenn the Geek</a> and <a href="http://www.drwendyying.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Dr. Wendy Ying</a> will be Emceeing the whole day with the help of <a href="http://www.horsesinthemorning.com/hosts/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">20 other Horse Radio Network (HRN) hosts.</a></p>
