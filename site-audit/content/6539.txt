<li>Your original order number.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<li>The quantity, style, colour, size and sole width of the boots you will be exchanging or returning (or relevant product info, if not boots).</li>

<li>Return Authorization Number must be clearly shown on the OUTSIDE of the returning parcel (near the address).</li>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo Exchange &amp; Return Policy Terms &amp; Conditions</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"backgroundColor":"light-red-1"} -->
<ul class="wp-block-list has-light-red-1-background-color has-background"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0)" class="has-inline-color has-red-color">PLEASE NOTE:</mark></strong> Orders made without first sending hoof measurement photos and obtaining a sizing recommendation from a Cavallo Customer Experience Expert will not be considered for a return shipping label.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>All products, packaging, and any promotional items originally included <strong>must be in AS NEW condition, ready for resale</strong>. These items will be returned to the shelf for the next customer (and must be just as you would expect when ordering them as new items yourself).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Boots returned to Cavallo without first arranging an exchange/return, obtaining a return authorization number, and submitting photos (to show the condition) will be returned at the sender’s expense, and no credit or refund will be given.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Boots that cannot be cleaned or returned to stock will not be exchanged or refunded and will be shipped back to the customer at their expense.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>For boots that can be returned to stock but require staff time to clean, the customer will only receive a 50% refund or value for exchange.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Cavallo includes free shipping on any new items for an exchange but does not cover the shipping costs of returned items.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Refunds and exchanges are provided anytime within 60 days after the initial purchase date.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>For returned items (that are not an exchange or warranty claim), a 10% restocking fee will be charged.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>When sending your returned item(s) back to Cavallo, please take care to ensure your parcel is protected against damage and/or loss and obtain a receipt with the tracking number. Cavallo can not accept responsibility for returned packages that are lost or damaged in transit.</li>

<p><mark style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0)" class="has-inline-color has-red-color"><strong>IMPORTANT: Riding or turning your horse out in boots will create some wear on the soles and, for this reason, will void eligibility for return or exchange.</strong></mark></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You must verify that your sizing is correct BEFORE use. Use our recommended Cavallo Fit Test, and/or contact Cavallo Customer Service right away if you have any questions or concerns about the fit.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Please be careful to follow these simple steps to keep your boots in “as new” condition when trying on:</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Place a plastic bag or saran wrap over the hoof so the inside of the boots stays perfectly clean.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Make sure you have a non-marking surface under the boot, such as cardboard, a rug, a doormat, or a towel.</li>
