<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Cavallo Trimmer Spotlight: Miriam Braun</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"fontSize":"large"} -->
<p class="has-large-font-size">Miriam Braun, Trimmer and Cavallo Associate Seller, shares about her journey in caring for horses with us:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px"><em>“At the age of 20 I was hired as a stable manager at a training facility with about 20 to 30 horses. The facility specialized in stallion training, dangerous horses, breeding, etc. Therefore, many horses were stall-bound and only had an hour turnout daily, kept in 24×12-foot runs, or larger sand paddocks in small groups. One of my responsibilities was overall care for all these horses. Observing the environments these horses lived in made it very clear that the environments had an enormous impact on hoof health, or lack thereof. With some obvious afflictions, we often turned to our main farrier.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"spacing":{"margin":{"top":"4px","bottom":"27px"}}}} -->
<p style="margin-top:4px;margin-bottom:27px"><em>We had a fabulous, Olympic-level farrier named Jim Peterson, who worked with most of the horses on the property. I was responsible for booking, prepping and handling the horses for Jim. And so as I watched, listened and enquired (satisfying my curiosity), it became clear that I had a deeper passion. It would take another four years before falling into another opportunity that gave me the way to becoming a professional hoof-care provider, but I never looked back!”</em> – <strong>Miriam Braun</strong>, Stoke (Estrie), Quebec, Canada</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Carole Herder, President and founder of Cavallo Horse &amp; Rider Inc., is an internationally acclaimed figure in the equestrian community. Her books, There Are No Horseshoes in Heaven and Hoof Prints on the Journey, have become international bestsellers, further solidifying her status as a thought leader in the industry.&nbsp;Since 1993, Carole has been deeply involved in horse health. Cavallo is a global leader in developing, manufacturing, and distributing horse products. Carole’s passion for horse comfort is evident in her designs, including the innovative Cavallo Hoof Boots and Total Comfort System Saddle Pads.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A true pioneer in the equestrian community, Carole is widely recognized for her expertise and contributions. She champions the benefits of keeping horses in their natural state, advocates for overall hoof health, and works towards the alleviation of debilitating disorders. Her achievements include being an honoured award recipient of the Royal Bank of Canada Woman Entrepreneur of the Year, British Columbia’s Businesswoman of The Year, and North America Enterprising Woman of The Year.&nbsp;<br>Carole is a certified Dr. Deepak Chopra Yoga, Meditation and Ayurvedic Lifestyle Instructor and a proud member of the Women Presidents Organization, supporting female entrepreneurs in every industry.</p>
