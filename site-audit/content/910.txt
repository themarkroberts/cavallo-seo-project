<p>I purchased a pair of SIMPLE Hoof Boots for use in the fall, winter, and early spring when my horse is barefoot. I did a 4 hour trail ride today with my new SIMPLE boots and was very happy with the way my horse performed with them. The trail ride consisted of rough terrain up and down hills. There were some paved and some graveled roads. We had to cross several streams, some with banks with sticky mud. The boots stayed on and did not twist or turn. My horse is known to stumble on trail rides, and a person commented on how he didn't stumble once today. The SIMPLE Boots did even better than I expected and by the end of the trail ride my confidence in them has soared.</p>
